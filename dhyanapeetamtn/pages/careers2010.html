Earn blissfully!

Can you achieve individual fulfillment in the inner world and success in the outer world at the same time? Can you work on pure inspiration and not be pushed by fear or greed? Can you earn a living as well as directly serve one and all?

If YES, here is a rare opportunity to contribute towards making an enlightened, awakened humanity!

 

The best gift you can get is to work for an enlightened master. He is the embodiment of the ultimate intelligence and compassion. So instead of the usual rat race which ends up in burnout or depression of success, here you have the blessing of being guided by the ultimate visionary.

Any action we do incurs karma corresponding to the intention with which the action was done. When we are driven by fear, greed, worry, etc., these emotions engrave their memories (samskaras) in us and add to our baggage of karma. The only way to get out of this vicious karma cycle is to do the action prescribed by the one whose intentions are in tune with the intention of Existence, Cosmos or God. Only an enlightened being can be in complete tune with Existence because He has no individual identity or ego.

 

Energy field

The place of work can’t be better than the energy field of the headquarters of Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam at Bengaluru. The ancient sacred healing banyan tree is the energy hub of the sacred space. The waters from the 21-feet Ananda Linga (healing Linga) in the Vaidya Sarovar (healing tank) have healed people from various ailments ranging from long-term psoriasis to arthritis. Above all, the presence of living enlightened master Paramahamsa Nithyananda is the ultimate blessing and protection in this heaven on planet earth.

Food and accommodation

Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam provides comfortable accommodation at the ashram. The ashram serves healthy, vegetarian, sattvic food to one and all. The peaceful ambience and the company of the lively enthusiastic full-time volunteers of the ashram will automatically inspire you to live enlightenment, be a jeevan mukta.
