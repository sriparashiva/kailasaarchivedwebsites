youth coordinators summit:-                                                                        

Kolli Hills ,5th and 6th July 2008:-

As most expected Nithyananda Yotuh Centre Coordinators Summit has been held at Kolli Hills on 5th and 6th of July 08.

The Minutes of the Event of (DAY 1)has been listed below[Day 2 Minutes and Pictures continues from the 4th Page].

 

*Nearly 42 co-ordinators and with 4 guests the bus starts traveling from Sendamangalam at 2.45am(05.07.08).
*The bus reached it’s destination (i.e.,KOLLI HILLS) at 5 am.
*Participants starts the bliss walk to Seekuparai view point at 6.30am.
*Everyone feels intense bliss to view the mountains wearing green dress and white caps .
*Simply we enjoyed the breeze of the bliss.
*On returning Mr.KANNAN karate master teaches the basics and importance of karate.
*Then participants had their Break Fast by 10am
*After breakfast the session of introducing each other starts.
*This session starts with warm welcome to special guest “Sri Nithya Saranagathananda Maharaj”.
*Maharaj gave special speech on the importance of youth foundation and shared his experiences which has made a spirit in everyone of us
*Maharaj invited youth members to participate in VAAZHUM VARALAARU program which was going to be conducted by SWAMIJI on August 1,2,3 at THIRUVANNAMALAI.
*After the session,we had our lunch with Maharaj.
*Next session was conducted by Mr. S.kannan (International Trainer) regarding Effective Public Speaking (E.P.S).
*Participants then visited the 9th century Lord Siva temple which was identified a few months ago.
*After that Participants went boating they enjoyed it.
*We had our dinner at 8.00 pm. 
*After that all Participants played an interesting game that is on the spot game.It was very much funny and entertaining which was followed by Camp Fire.
