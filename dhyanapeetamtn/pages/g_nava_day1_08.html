Navarathiri celebrations:-  

DAY 1 - NYF Head Office, Namakkal - 31st Sep, 2008:-

Navarathiri Celebrations started at the Nithyananda Youth Foundation Head Office, Namakkal. Day 1 celebrations comprises of the Laksharchana which is Chanting the guru mantra One Lakh Times.

 

Ones whole inner chatter becomes the mantra, so that when a thought arises, be it negative or positive, the whole flavor of the thought is transformed now with the  fragrance of the

mantra. As we chant this mantra the divine energy starts filling our inner space melting away the engraved memories and the 'I' and the 'mine'.
