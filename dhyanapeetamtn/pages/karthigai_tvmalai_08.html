karthigai deepam:-                                                                            

Thiruvannamalai Ashram, 11th December 2008:-

On the full moon day of the lunar month of 'Karthigai' 'Karthigai Deepam' is celebrated the world over. It is on this day, when the sun is about to set and the full moon has just risen on the eastern horizon; a huge beacon is lit on the holy hill Arunachala in Tiruvannamalai located in southern India. The sun represents the right eye, the moon the left eye and the fire represents the 'third eye'. The lighting of the beacon represents the awakening 'ajna chakra' of a person. It is the awareness created by this awakened energy that serves as the light that guides us through every problem that we face in our lives.It is on this day, when Goddess Devi attained the state of Enlightenment and became part of Shiva.
