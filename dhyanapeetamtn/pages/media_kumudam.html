Tamil Weekly Magazine ' Kumudam ' publishes Swamiji's talks in an article series titled
' Athmavai Thira Anandam Pongattum... '

1. Go to www.kumudam.com
2. In the login box, sign in with username: magazine.tamil@gmail.com and password: nithyanandam. (Also, you     may create a registration ID for yourself and use that to sign in.)
3. Once you are signed in, click on the View Magazine >> Link under the heading 'Kumudam'
4. In the screen that loads, menu will be seen on the left side bar. Do mouse over on the menu called     'Thodarkal' and Click on the sub menu 'Paramahamsa Sri Nithyanandar' to view the article
