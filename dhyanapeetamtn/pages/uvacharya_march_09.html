Yuvacharya training program:-                                                                                                 

Rajapalayam Ashram - 28th & 29th Mar 2009:-

Yuvacharya Training Program - a 2 day residential program for the training of Youth Achrayas(From the members of Nithyananda International Youth Foundation[NIYF] ) has been conducted at Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam, Rajapalayam Ashram on 28 & 29th March 2009. Around 200 Youth Members had participated in the two day event.
