BSP in Ohio - 2008
On August 31, 2008, for the first time in the USA Bhakti Spurana Program (BSP) was conducted by Sri Nithya Bhaktananda Swami. The participants enjoyed the flowering of Bhakti at the Ohio Vedic Temple.
