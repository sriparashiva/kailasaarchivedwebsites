Nithyananda's 30th Birthday Celebration - Jan 1, 2007 in Los Angeles

Los Angeles, CA

Los Angeles, CA | Columbus, OH

Nithyananda's 30th Birthday was celebrated with great pomp and splendor on January 1, 2007 in all Nithyananda mission centers worldwide. In Los Angeles, the headquarters of Life Bliss Foundation, celebration started with a special prayer and meditation in the morning ushering in joy and positivity. The festivities were topped off with a grand celebration in the evening.

The celebration in Columbus, OH was is the new Meditation Center amidst great joy. Kids and adults alike had a blissful time. The celebreation started on December 31, 2006 with the ckae being cut at midnight. Delicious Dinner, Dance for kirthans, Musical chair were followed by Midnight Cake cutting for Jayanthi by our Little Anandas ,toast with Apple juice and ushered in the New year with a prayer and group meditation. As the grand finale, fire crackers were filled the night sky with colors....
