USA Kumba Mela - Sept' 9, 2007 in Los Angeles

USA Kumba Mela 2007 was held on Sunday, September 9 in Los Angeles under the auspices of Paramahamsa Nithyananda – Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam and Swami Sarvadevananda - Vedanta Society. More than 20 Southern California spiritual organizations took part in the festival, which featured a half-mile procession with an estimated 7,000 chanting marchers who carried pictures and images of deities and spiritual leaders from various temples. 

The program started with a jubilious celebration in the form of procession which was filled with fun and festivity and devotees chanting, singing and dancing dressed in different costumes to entertain the huge crowd that had gathered to watch the procession. The procession featured 10 Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam temples from the United States and representatives from Vedanata society, BAPS, ISKON, Mata Amrutandamayi foundation, Siddha Yoga, Siddha Samadhi yoga, Sanathana Dharma temple, Sindi Center, Hamsa Yoga, Gujarathi Association and many others. Swami Vishwananda from Bhakti Marga Foundation also graced the occasion with his presence. At the end of the procession the immersion pool was sanctified with water from 21 holy rivers of India, before the idols were immersed in the holy water to the sacred chants from the Vedas. 

Paramahamsa Nithyananda, expounding on the significance of the Kumba Mela being held in the United States, said the spirit of the Kumba Mela is loving and sharing – a reminder of respect and brotherhood among the various spiritual traditions. Swamiji said there is an urgent need to raise the collective positive energy and spread the message of peace and brotherhood in this country. Research proves that when so many people collectively pray for peace, the same kind of thought waves, nullifies the negative energy and promotes positive energy in the universe. Swamiji ended his talk with a prayer "Let the positive energy created today, create peace inside each of us, create peace in our houses and ultimately peace in the whole country. God Bless America". The talks by all the dignitaries present ended with Arati to all the deities present. Cultural performances from various organizations went on until late in the night.
