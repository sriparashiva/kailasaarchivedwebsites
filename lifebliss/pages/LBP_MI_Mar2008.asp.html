LBP in Michigan - Mar 28th - 30th 2008
Mar 28th to 30th, 2008, marked a blissful weekend in Waterford, Michigan. A free talk introducing the Life Bliss Program was given on the 28th, and the Life bliss Program itself was conducted on 29th and 30th. The program was well attended, and all the participants were filled with bliss and gratitude when they dispersed at the end of the workshop.
