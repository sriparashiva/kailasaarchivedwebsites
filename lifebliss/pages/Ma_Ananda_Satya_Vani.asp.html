Ma Nithya Gnanapriyananda

St. Louis, Mo.
abalaram@charter.net

Ma Nithya Gnanapriyananda has been truly blessed to be one among the first group of Acharyas that have been appointed by Sri Paramahamsa Nithyananda in 2004. Her intense search for a Guru made her stay in Ashrams and study under several Masters. For many years she practiced Pranic Healing and Kriya Yoga and attended retreats conducted by Spiritual Leaders.

Attending the first Life Bliss Program conducted in St. Louis by Paramahamsa Nithyananda completely revolutionized her path to spirituality. Hence became His devotee and attended all His meditation programs and Acharya workshops.

She has been a city coordinator in St. Louis since 2004. By His grace as an Acharya she has been regularly conducting classes in Life Bliss Program, Health And Wellness, Nithya Dhyaan and Nithyananda Spurana Program
