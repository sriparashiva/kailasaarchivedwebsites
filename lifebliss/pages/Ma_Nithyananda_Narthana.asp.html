Ma Nithyananda Narthana moved from her native country of Slovenia to California in 1985. She has been practicing many styles of Hatha Yoga and meditation since early years. Meeting her spiritual Master, Paramahamsa Nithyananda, in 2005 changed her life profoundly. Becoming a healer and a teacher (Acharya) for Life Bliss Foundation has given her the opportunity to fully embody the spiritul wisdom and radiate the bliss. It is her passion to spread Nithyananda's message of meditation to the world.

In her own words:" My whole life has become a meditation. Whether it is working in my profession as a graphic designer or cooking a healthy meal for my family. I simply dance through life."

With my deepest gratitude and love
