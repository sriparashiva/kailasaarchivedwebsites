Nithyananda Spurana Program (NSP) - Nov 18,19 2006 in Los Angeles

 

A historic event...

The first Nithyananda Spurana Program (NSP) conducted by a Mahacharya (Senior Teacher) in North America was conducted in Los Angeles on November 18, 19 2006. The venue was Oak Tree Inn Conference Room, 788 W Huntington Dr,Monrovia, CA.

Nithyananda Spurana Program (NSP) is a two-day meditation camp where participants have the rare opportunity to receive instruction and inspiration and explore in depth the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of our nature. This is an opportunity to consciously decide to fall in tune with our True Self.

Till recently, this program was taught directly by the Master Himself. Recently he has ordained a handful of Mahacharyas to conduct this intense workshop.
