Nithyananda Spurana Program Schedule

The last Nithyananda Spurana Program (NSP) of this year will happen in Los Angeles, CA on November 18/19, conducted by a Mahacharya (Senior Teacher), specially ordained by Swamiji. This is a tremendous chance to surrender your pains, fears, sorrows, desires, guilt and pleasures to Existence. Live them out with as much intensity as you dare to in the presence of the Existential Energy and feel them dissolving.

  • Rewind your life and confess like you have never done before.
  • Detach yourself from your deep rooted and unconscious feelings.
  • Rejuvenate your Being and get ready to be reborn with tremendous mental clarity.

Limited seats available!

Register and reserve your spot, Now!

7 scientifically laid out meditation techniques, designed by the Master will be taught. These techniques bring about great intellectual clarity for living and help us conquer the pain and fear of death, making it a celebration.
Get ready with all your questions on Life and Death – your answers are on their way!

Get rid of your samskaras!!!
Conquer the fear of death!!!
Immerse your Life in Bliss!!!
