Areas Of Study in the 15 day training

  1. Patanjali and basic understanding of Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras

  2. Nithyananda’s concept of the mind

  3. Nithyananda’s concept ‘Be Unclutched’

  4. Mind/Body connection

  5. Study into scientific research on power of intention and the mind

  6. Chakras, Bodies, Koshas

  7. Teaching Nithya Dhyaan (Life Bliss Meditation)

  8. Performing Guru Puja

  9. Basic Sanskrit and pronunciation (Guru Puja mantra, Nithya Surya Namaskar Mantra)

  10. Nithya Yoga for Body

  11. Nithya Yoga for Being (Enlightenment)

  12. Nithya Yoga for Little Ananda’s (Kids Yoga)

  13. Teaching Krishnamacharya’s Vinyasa Krama

  14. Teaching Pranayama

  15. Mudra

  16. Guided relaxation and gratitude meditation

  17. Basics of Ayurveda
