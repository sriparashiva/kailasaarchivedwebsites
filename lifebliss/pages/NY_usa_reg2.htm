Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I have to have any experience in teaching or practicing yoga before I attend the training? 
No. You simply need an open mind. Prior experience in yogic practices is not required.

2. Are there any pre-requisites for the Nithya yoga Teachers training program? 
Yes. Nithyananda's first level program - LBP level 1

3. How much does the training cost?
The 15 day intensive program offered by the Nithyananda Yoga Foundation will cost $1500. This does not include accommodation.

4. If I attend a Nithya Yoga Teachers Training, am I guaranteed of ordainment? (Will I be allowed to teach straight away?)
At the conclusion of the Nithya Yoga Teachers Training, there will be an ordainment ceremony where it will be decided whether you will be ordained as a 'Nithya Yogacharya' or whether you will me made an 'Assistant Teacher of Nithya Yoga.' If you are ordained as a Nithya Yogacharya at the ordainment ceremony, this means you can start giving introductory talks on Nithya Yoga and start teaching the Nithya Yoga session to the public immediately.

If you are made an Assistant Teacher of Nithya Yoga, this means that you cannot teach Nithya Yoga immediately and independently & to the public.

If you are made as an Assistant Teacher of Nithya Yoga at the first training you attend, it has been identified that you need more time to internalize Nithyananda's teachings and concepts before presenting them to the public. As an Assistant Teacher of Nithya Yoga, you are still given the opportunity to internalize the concepts and practice teaching the various yogic techniques by assisting the ordained Nithya Yogacharyas during classes conducted in your area. Under the supervision of Nithya Yogacharyas, you can assist in their Nithya Yoga classes and with their discretion and guidance, teach the various Nithya Yoga dhyana, asana, pranayama and mudra.

5. Can you earn money from teaching Nithya Yoga?
The current agreement is that Nithya Yogacharyas can keep 60% of what they make from Nithya Yoga classes. The remaining 40% is offered to the foundation.

6. If I don't become ordained as a Nithya Yogacharya at my first training, am I allowed to come back and attend another training?
Yes. You are allowed to attend all subsequent Nithya Yoga Teacher Trainings until you are ordained by Nithyananda as a Nithya Yogacharya. These subsequent trainings you can attend free of charge.

7. If I become a Nithya Yogacharya, will I be issued a certificate or be given a document to certify that I can teach?
Yes, you will be issued a certificate immediately upon ordainment.

8. Can we teach Nithya Yoga to the general public or must we request people to do LBP or ASP first?
Nithya Yoga can be taught to the general public. Having completed LBP is not a requirement for one to attend. People attending a Nithya Yoga class may actually become more inspired to attend the LBP.
