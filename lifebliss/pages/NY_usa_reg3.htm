Testimonials

The Nithya Yoga Teacher Training (NYTT) is like nothing else I've ever done. I went in with no expectations, just drawn to attend it. I made the decision to go to it, and did! My life will never be the same. Such amazing experiences I had as I practiced the daily meditations and guru puja, mantras and asana practice. Not only was I learning how to share this with others, but I was transforming every moment along the way, thereby able to share with others my direct experiences with Nithya Yoga. Having been a yoga instructor for 7 years prior to this training, I thought I knew something about yoga. In the NYTT not only did I learn about true, real, pure Yoga, but experienced it for myself. Every moment can be a practice of Yoga...this was my experience at the NYTT. Yoga, the Uniting of Oneself with Divinity can be experienced in this lifetime...even on this day! My deepest Gratitude to you Paramahamsa Nithyananda (Swamiji)!

- Ma Nithyananda Nischala (Phoenix)

The Nithya Yoga Teachers Training Program was certainly one of the most life-transforming and memorable experiences for me ever. We were a bunch of us from 20 different countries, but we did everything together in the Nithya Yoga way! We literally lived Nithya Yoga all through the fifteen days of training, our routine consisted of yogic cleansing, eating yogic food in yogic style, Nithya Yoga sessions, discussions, yogic sleep (yoga nidra)... we literally talked, walked and breathed yoga! This deep process has helped me to incorporate and internalize yoga every day, every moment! My deepest gratitude to Swamiji and the NY acharyas for making this happen. 

- Ma Nithyananda Arpana (Singapore)

Nithya Yoga Teachers Training (NYTT) is an all-in-one course. We learnt yoga in its most prisitine form but more than that the 15 days of stay was itself a blissful learning experience. 40 odd people from 11 different countries - that was the most beautiful part. Different cultures, different languages, different life styles - all converged into one lifestyle - the Nithya Yogic life style, during the 15 days. The food we ate, the drinks we drank, the movements we made - everything was the Nithya Yogic way. Each one of us was totally blissfully enjoying each and every moment of our stay which is exactly what Nithya Yoga is all about - being in the present moment in totality!

- Sri Nithya Arpanananda (Singapore)

Nithya Yoga Teachers Training transformed my life. The immediate impact covered many areas. I was able to go deeply inward and let go of some of the issues holding me back. I lost weight and gained tremendous energy. By living for 10 days, 24/7, eating, breathing, being yoga, I found what I was looking for. So many parts of my daily practice in teachers training I implemented into my daily life and without effort, continue to practice 8 months later. The theoretical part of the training really clarified and anchored into my being - the teaching of Patanjali. By far the most powerful experience was experiencing the state of yoga. Being in that state has had a ripple effect in my life that I am still experiencing 8 months later. 

It is a blessing to be able to deliver the teachings of Nithya Yoga to my students and so fulfilling and rewarding to see them transform. In deepest gratitude.

- Ma Nithyananda Bodhaana (Columbus, Ohio)
