Grand Inauguration & Consecration of Dhyanapeetam Anandeshwara Temple in Columbus, Ohio

Grand Maha Kumbabishekam and Kumbha Mela will be held from Apr'12th to Apr'15th, to celebrate the inauguration of Dhyanapeetam Anandeshwara Temple in Columbus, Ohio for the first time ever by an Enlightened Master in the Americas! Everyone is welcome to join us in celebrating this historic event!

Venue:

Dhyanapeetam Anandeshwara Temple

820 Pollock Rd,
Delaware OH 43015
