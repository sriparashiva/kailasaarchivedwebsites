Satsang's in France - April 2008
Seen below  are some pictures taken during different Satsang's happening in Albi, South of France, every Sundays afternoon. A large number of people are getting attracted to Paramahamsa Nithyananda's teachings and meditation. The French center has an active Satsang every weekend with people coming regularly at different times to receive healing and to practice Swamiji’s meditation techniques.
