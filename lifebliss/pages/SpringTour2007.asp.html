Atma Spurana Workshop - Mar 24,25 2007

Paramahamsa Nithyananda arrived in Los Angeles on February 26, 2007. During the upcoming Spring Tour, Nithyananda will be delivering public discoursesand conducting meditation workshops in 8 cities across the United States and Canada.

In the evening, the first public darshan was held in the new meditation hall/temple at the ashram. A huge number of devotees from all over the country gathered to welcome Nithyananda and receive his blessings.

Nithyananda delivered 2 pulic discourses in San Diego on Mar 2 and 3 2007. Nithyananda also started Nithya Dhyan Initiation at the 2nd discourse in San Diego for the first time in the United States. A large number of enthusiastic devotees received the initiation on this day.

The Jain Sutras delivered by Mahavir, the last of the 24 recognized Tirthankaras, contains the knowledge that leads to Self-Realization, with power to awaken man to his true nature, to awaken the divinity within, to bring forth the inner bliss that is trying to express every minute! They show the torchlight towards attaining fulfillment in both inner and outer worlds. Paramahamsa Nithyananda is revisiting the truths from Jain Sutras reiterating and elaborating them in his own inimitable, dynamic style at the Jain Temple & Cultural Center in Beuna Park, CA.
