Sri Ananda Sarvasri -- I began my spiritual quest in 1971 when first introduced to meditation. It opened up in me a whole new world of possibilities for spiritual enlightenment. I began in earnest, generally meditating 8-12 hours per day, continuing for 10-12 years or more... rarely practicing less than four hours per day. During these years I read beautiful and inspiring stories in the ancient scriptures, the Srimad Bhagavata Mahapurana, Bhagavad Gita, Ramacharitamanasa of Tulasidas (Ramayana), Upanishads, Yoga Sutras, and others. I studied the original Christian teachings, as well as other traditions, always trying to discover, to experience -- what is the ultimate truth? How do I realize the enlightened divine connection?

Though some progress was made, really, all this experience beautifully shows the truth Nithyananda expresses when he says that the old classical techniques do not work so well with the modern western mind. With what I knew, with the old techniques, I could not progress beyond a certain point. I was not alone. Practically everyone I knew, or knew of, practicing similar methods did not achieve the divine connection, the state of bliss that was predicted. Where was the bliss? The search had to continue.

Searching the internet led to the discovery of Paramahamsa Nithyananda. The first meeting confirmed that here was a being who could truly help us. What is even more precious, is that he wants to help us. The radiance of bliss simply explodes from him. I had read about such beings from the ancient past, but I did not know that such a being existed today. I didn't believe such a being could exist in human form! Not only his presence, but his teaching expresses such intense enlightenment. His teaching is so absolutely clear and produces remarkable, dramatic transformation into bliss. He manifests this bliss and compassion in greater measure than I thought possible. How could I resist? .

It is such a deep joy for me to offer Nithyananda's teaching to others. The breadth and depth of his understanding, the lightness of his expression, the the variety of approaches he offers is such an amazement to me. I feel so fortunate and so grateful to be able to offer his programs of meditation. I can only suggest to anyone to give these methods and understandings a practical trial. See how much your life can be opened into health, bliss, peace, compassion, devotion, and all beautiful things. .

Sri Ananda Sarvasri currently works as a natural healer, employing many different healing disciplines. He spends as much time as possible spreading the teachings of Nithyananda.
