Sri Nithya Jyotirmayananda - commonly called as Sri Jyotir is a Los Angeles Ashramite. Life hasn't been the same ever since he met Swamiji a few years ago in the LA Ashram inauguration. It has been an amazing blissful ride that cannot be described in mere words or just in an introduction. He and his wife have the privilege of being the first couple whose marriage was conducted by Swamiji Himself (in July 2006). He teaches the weekend meditation workshops and Nithya Yoga. He also work as a Software Engineer in Microsoft, where he has given a number of lectures and seminars on meditation and its positive effects on corporate and day-to-day life.
