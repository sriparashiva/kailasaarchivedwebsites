Sri Nithyananda Dheera is an internationally known leadership trainer and expedition leader. He is the first person in the world to lead expeditions to the three extremes - Everest and the North and South Poles. Nithyananda Dheera has gone on to use his leadership skills to teach at the highest levels of business and non-profit organizations.

He met Nithyananda in 2005 and immediately recognized the amazing abilities of this enlightened master and since then has been teaching courses for the Life Bliss Foundation. He is now an Ashramite in LA ashram and is Director of the Ananda Sevak Program.
