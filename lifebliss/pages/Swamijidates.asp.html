The schedule of all of Swamiji's programs organized by Life Bliss Foundation for the year 2007 is as follows. Click to see Swamiji's full calendar.
Paramahamsa Nithyananda dwells upon and demystifies the ancient scriptures in the language of now and here. He brings out its relevance in the context of your own life. He urges one to move on from understanding to experience.
Nithyananda is visiting United States and Canada in Spring 2007. Soak in the Words of Wisdom from the Master, Meditate in his presence...

Nithya Spiritual Healing Prayer Initiation(Self Healing)

In Spring Tour 2007, the Nithya Spiritual Healing Prayer Initiation (offered by Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam, India) is only for Self Healing. To perform healing prayer services for another person, you are required to participate in another 2-day Advanced Nithya Spiritual Healing Prayer Initiation program.

If you are:

* currently an Active Nithya Spiritual Healer:
You can continue to practice healing prayer services for yourself and othes.

* a New Nithya Spiritual Healer (Self Healing):
After the Self-healing Initiation, you may practice healing prayer only for your self, till you attend an Advanced Nithya Spiritual Healers' Initiation program.

Pre-requisites

  • You should have attended an advanced workshop (NSP, DSP or Atma Spurana) conducted by Paramahamsa Nithyananda
  • You should have attended or commit to attend LBP - the basic workshop on 7 major energy centers (chakras) in our body.

    • Registration at Atma Spurana workshop or at the venue for Nithya Spiritual Healing Prayer Initiation (Self Healing) in the city of your choice
