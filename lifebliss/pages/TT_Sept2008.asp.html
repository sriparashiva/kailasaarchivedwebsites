Acharya Retreat and Teacher Training Oct 31st to Nov 8th
Is an amazing opportunity to dramatically deepen your experience of the teachings of Nithyananda, and prepare to share these teachings with the world.

The training takes place at the Los Angeles center in Montclair, California. Senior Acharya's including Sri Nithya Medhananda will be leading this session. 

This intensive training involves learning the material delivered at the Life Bliss Program, and imbibing the teachings so that they become part of your very nature. This is a beautiful pathway to being the most profound conduit of Nithyananda's energy. Swamiji says "Acharya's are my mouth." What happens at this training cannot be precisely described in words, however many people have experienced the massive transformation that takes place. No prior teaching experience is necessary. Just an attitude of openness to transformation is necessary.

This training is mandatory to attend for current and aspiring acharya'a, and is also a prerequisite to attend to be able to attend the teachers training with Swamiji

Cost: $500 excluding accommodation

  • Taken a Life bliss Program
  • Read Guaranteed Solutions 6 times
  • Watch a set of 7 training videos 3 times

    Write as though your are speaking to the group your presentation about the chakra. Please keep this information as close to the wording of Guaranteed Solutions as possible.
