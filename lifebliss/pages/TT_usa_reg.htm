Welcome to the Nithya Yoga Teachers Training Program

Nithyanandam!!!

Yoga is a vast ocean. Yoga is for everyone. Different kinds of people practice some form of yoga these days for many different reasons. 

Some people like to come along to a yoga class to gain physical strength, balance, and flexibility in the body. 

Some like to attend a yoga class in the hope of being healed, gaining mental clarity, peace of mind and heightened awareness. 

Some come along to a yoga class understanding that yoga is an ancient science designed to raise our consciousness; and it is a practice designed to prepare the body and mind to receive, sustain and express the energy of Enlightenment. 

Which ever way your students want to approach a yoga practice, whether it be for the physical body, to calm the mind or for Enlightenment itself, as a Nithya Yogacharya (teacher), you will be able to share all 3 levels and understandings with your students: 

- Nithya Yoga for Body
- Nithya Yoga for Mind 
- Nithya Yoga for Being (Enlightenment)

Areas of study in the 15 day training

- Patanjali and basic understanding of Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras
- Nithyananda’s concept of the mind
- Nithyananda’s concept ‘Be Unclutched’
- Mind/Body connection
- Chakras, Bodies, Koshas
- Teaching Nithya Dhyan (Life Bliss Meditation)
- Performing Guru Puja
- Basic Sanskrit and pronounciation (Guru Puja mantra, Nithya Surya Namaskar Mantra)
- Nithya Yoga for Body
- Nithya Yoga for Mind
- Nithya Yoga for Being (Enlightenment)
- Nithya Yoga for Little Ananda’s (Kids Yoga)
- Teaching Vinyasa Krama, developing personalized vinyasa sequences
- Teaching Pranayama
- Mudra
- Guided relaxation and gratitude meditation
- Basics of Ayurveda - Guest Ayurvedic Professor
