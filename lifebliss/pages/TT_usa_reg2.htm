How to prepare to come to the MTP2007…..

General Requirements:

Programs:

Each participant must have attended a 2 day program or an educational travel program with Swamiji.

Daily practice:

      Participants are requested to prepare by following these 3 principles:

  • Nithyam:

Everyone needs to be conversant and aware of the philosophy and teachings of the master in clear terms. Familiarity with the messages in book Guaranteed Solutions and watching the video clips on the www.YouTube.com/LifeBlissFoundation is a good start. Please spend a few hours either reading one of Swamiji’s books, Watching videos, or in the satsang every day.

  • Dhyanam:

Please do Nithya Dhyaan meditation every day.

  • Anandam:

Please do some work for the mission, with out bothering about personal gain! Where it does not matter as to who gets the credit, a lot is achieved. The Ananda happens.

Special requirements

Ananda Sevaks (Fulltime Volunteers) Training Program:

Ananda Sevaks choose service as their spiritual path and dedicate their life to the mission work and are expected to be prepared with the following:

·        Participant must have attended the 2day Life Bliss Program.

·        Participant must be clear about the concepts in the book Guaranteed Solutions. There will be a quiz to test the participant's knowledge.

·        Dedication - Participant should be prepared for fulltime commitment to the program

·        Devotion - Participant should feel a strong connection with the Master and his mission.

·        Visionary - Participant should be willing to change and adapt to the changes which may happen as part of the spiritual process.

·        Missionary - Participant should be prepared to work extended hours of training in spiritual practices.

·        Monk - Participant should be willing to relocate if required as part of the training or work.

Ananda Karya Kartas (Volunteers) Training Program:

Ananda Karya Kartas are expected to be prepared with the following:

·        Participant must have attended the 2day Life Bliss Program.

·        Participant must be familiar with Guaranteed Solutions.

·        Participant should feel a strong connection with the Master and his mission.

·        Participant should arrive with a list of ideas to contribute to the mission. Participant may prepare and bring proposals to present to the group.

Ananda Pramukhas (Organisers) Training Program:

Pramukhas are requested to be prepared with the following:

·         Participant must have attended the 2day Life Bliss Program.

·         Participant must be familiar with Guaranteed Solutions. There will be an interview to test the participant's ability to answer questions.

·     Participant must have gone through and internalized Swamiji's 274+ clips on www.YouTube.com/LifeblissFoundation . There will be a quiz to check the participant's awareness of these breakthrough messages.

·        Participant must be able to answer any type of question on Life Solutions and the ancient wisdom Swamiji has spoken about. The best method to do this is to thoroughly read, listen and "internalize" all the books and discourses of Swamiji.

·        Participant must be very good in presenting the concepts in a convincing and inspiring way. This shall be measured and honed primarily during the Pramukhas’ training program.

·        Participant must make a strong commitment to inspiring people and continuously update themselves on the latest from Swamiji and Foundation.

·        Participant should arrive with ideas for spreading of the mission in the center and beyond. Participant is requested to please bring along proposals in the form of presentations, for marketing and campaign of the programs including sound fundraising and financial plans.  

Acharya training Program:

Every devotee who wishes to be an Acharya will have to go through this program. There are several changes to the Life Bliss program which will be released and training imparted in these 9 days. All existing and past Acharyas will need to satisfy the following requirements in order to be ordained and continue as Acharyas.

The following are some of the requirements for participating in the Acharya training program:

·        Participant must have attended a 2 day program with Swamiji and the 2day Life Bliss Program.

·        Participant must be very thorough with Guaranteed Solutions. There will be a quiz to test the participant's knowledge.

·     Participant must have gone through and internalized Swamiji's 274+ clips on www.YouTube.com/LifeblissFoundation . There will be a quiz to check the participant's awareness of these breakthrough messages.

·        Participant must be able to answer any type of question on Life Solutions and the ancient wisdom Swamiji has spoken about. The best method to do this is to thoroughly read, listen and "internalize" all the books and discourses of Swamiji.

·        Participant must be very good in presenting the concepts in a convincing and inspiring way. This shall be measured and honed primarily during the teacher's training program.

·        Participant must make a strong commitment to teaching & inspiring people and continuously update themselves on the latest from Swamiji and Foundation.

·        Participant must arrive at the training with complete preparation.

 There will be an initial screening for acharyas on the day 1, day 2.  The participants will be assigned appropriate line of training based on the aptitude and attitude. In fact this will be the blessing form the Master for your spiritual growth.

 When the Master decides to have some one serve as an acharya, it is Prasad. When the Master decides to have some one serve in any capacity it is a Prasad. It is a selection not a rejection. Aspiring and working to be an acharya is devotion. Embracing the Prasad with gratitude is divinity.
