Sri Nithya Bhaktananda Swami

Email: ny@nithyananda.org
Ph: 626-716-7463

Sri Nithya Bhaktananda Swami was a successful career professional with businesses in India and in the USA. He has a BE, MBA, PGDCA, PGDSM and worked as an executive in India Government for a decade. He has been a enthusiastic member of several Social service and professional organizations. He moved to the USA several years ago and worked as as a consultant for large corporations, including Deliotte, Price Water, Oracle etc. At a later stage he also founded a couple of Consulting firms. 

Nithya Bhaktananda met the master Paramahamsa Nithyananda in California. Instantaneously, he fell in love with the Paramahamsa and felt the divine calling. Soon the Master initiated him into his graceful fold and personally groomed the disciple from a typical stressed corporate professional into a healing Spiritual Counselor. Nithya Bhaktananda had the privilege of Paramahamsa’s personal tutelage for 4 years.

Having had a beautiful family life, Nithya Bhaktananda is passionate and very successful in mentoring children and youth and restoring bliss in troubled families through counseling and several other programs. He takes particular interest in Juvenile children and broken families. 

With his inspiring messages from the Paramahamsa’s life, Nithya Bhaktananda is popular all over the world as a spiritual leader, counselor, healer and a very practical teacher. He has also conducted several advanced meditation programs and Teachers Training programs in several countries, for the Nithyananda Mission. He is a motivational and spiritual speaker at several places including corporations like Microsoft, Oracle, Petrobras, Parle Foods, PowerGrid, and several Yoga and health organizations like 24HrFitness, Prolimits, GoldGym etc.
