Thanksgiving Carnival - Nov 19, 2006 in Los Angeles

To celebrate Thankgiving, Life Bliss Foundation organized a carnival at the Arcadia Park on Nov 19, 2006. The weather turned out perefect and so did the day! Around 80 members of the Nithyananda family from in and around Los Angeles gathered for the event. It was a day of FUN, FOOD, LOVE AND BLISS for young and old alike...
