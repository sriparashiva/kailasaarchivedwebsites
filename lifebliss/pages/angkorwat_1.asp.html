July 1-7 2007: A sacred journey to Angkor Wat

A fund raising trip in support of the North American Headquartres of the mission

  • A 7 day trip to Angkor Wat, Cambodia with the Master from 1 July '07 to 7 July '07
  • An opportunity to go beyond body and mind and experience pure Consciousness.
  • This trip is being arranged as part of the fund-raising initiative for the North American Headquarter of the Mission which encompasses building a temple and a Yoga & Meditation University in Los Angeles. Complete payment information will be provided upon receiving your application

Those who are interested, may apply for this program. On approval of your application an invitation will be sent to you for the sacred journey.

Payment details will be posted shortly.
