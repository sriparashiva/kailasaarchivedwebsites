The Life Bliss Tree

Amid the Guru Poornima festivities on July 11 2006 The Bliss Tree activities were launched simultaneously in 8 cities across US and in Vancouver,Canada

The Bliss Tree is a chain of concept-stores being introduced by Life Bliss Foundation.

The Life Bliss Tree complex will have two main branches under the same roof. Each branch aims at enriching an individuals inner and outer worlds through the various Ananda products and services being made available. One branch of the Bliss Tree will nourish the body, mind and spirit through the offerings of yoga and meditation sessions, while the second branch is a store carrying a selection of eclectic products.
