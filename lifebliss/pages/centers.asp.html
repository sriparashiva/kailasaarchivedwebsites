Centers around the world

The spiritual hub of the mission is Dhyanapeetam , Bangalore, India . The Western headquarters were established in Los Angeles, CA in September 2005. All centers and meditation academies are "spiritual laboratories" which utilize time-tested Vedic inputs and modern technology; they are a place where inner growth of the individual is profound and outer growth a natural outcome. The academies are envisioned to be a place where one will be able to explore the inner world and explode into the outer, through a host of activities where meditation meets science. They will offer opportunities for quantum spirituality, where material and spiritual worlds merge and create blissful living; where creative intelligence stems from deep Consciousness.

In the pipeline are several projects at the various centers worldwide. New centers continue to be established rapidly, in order to provide services in various fields to humanity at large.

Established in the year 2003 and now spanning 24 countries, the mission continues to transform humanity through the transformation of the individual.
