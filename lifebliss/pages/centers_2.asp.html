India

Dhyanapeetam, India with headquarters in Bangalore is the spiritual nerve center of the mission. Nestling among the low hills and spread over several acres of land, the ashram as it is referred to is located in Bidadi near Bangalore, Karnataka. The ashram is resonant with the energy radiated by the Master. Exuding mysticism and equipped with modern amenities, this is a space where mere existence is meditation.

The energy hub of the ashram is a sacred Banyan tree. Revealed to the Master in his vision and worshipped as the guiding spirit of the ashram, this sacred banyan encloses a space resonant with the Existential energy. Nithyananda says, "This space is like an airstrip for the spirit to soar and experience the profoundity of Existence itself.

Dhyanapeetam ashrams are located in Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh and Salem, Tamilnadu as well with more upcoming projects spread all over the country.

For more information, please visit http://www.dhyanapeetam.org.
