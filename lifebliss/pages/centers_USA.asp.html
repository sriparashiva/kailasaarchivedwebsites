Centers in the U.S.

The Western headquarters of the mission is in Los Angeles, CA. The foundation has 8 'Life Bliss Tree'(s) in the United States which are concept stores offering ecelectic products and services related to yoga, meditation and spirituality.

In addition to these, several 5 star centers are active all over the country. All 5 star centers provide the following 5 services -

  • Life Bliss Program Workshops
  • Ananda Galleria
  • Weekly satsangs
  • Free Nithya Spiritual Healing and Prayer(Healing is provided by spiritual healers of Dhyanapeetam)
  • Organization of the Master's visits and programmes

To locate the 5-star center in your neighborhood, contact the coordinator in the nearest city.
