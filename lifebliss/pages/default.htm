Little Anandas

Celebrating innocence

We are pleased to announce the grand opening of Little Anandas – Celebrating Innocence (the Weekend Cultural School) on the auspicious day of Gurupoornima 2007. The curriculum includes various meditations, chanting of mantras, sanskrit shlokas, yoga, rituals, Indian music, Indian dances, reading and writing of devnagari script, Indian languages and celebration of Indian festivals. The festival celebrations are the big events to look forward to for everyone.

Life is Education, Expression and Celebration. Allowing children to discover the truth by being spontaneous and creative helps them understand things for themselves. This enhances their creative capacity for life; and creativity becomes their wisdom. This capacity and wisdom will lead them to unchartered sea of truth. Little Anandas is a program to bring children closer to existence and experience the sweetness of education through love, meditation, celebration and creativity. Children are introduced to the Art of Centering through meditations, the Enriching of the Self through creativity, and the Expanding of Self -Awareness through self-imposed fun based challenges. Education here means drawing the inner being into expression in your day-to-day life.

At Little Anandas, children learn the basic values of Vedic culture. The Vedic tradition is a constant reminder, a constant inspiration to work on the inner world, to transform and to experience a different space, a different world. Only Vedic renaissance can give the experience of spiritual truth, the experience of the inner world, the supreme consciousness.

The program will be conducted on Saturdays and Sundays at Vedic Temple, Montclair. More locations are coming soon. Please contact Vedic Temple at (909) 625-1400 to find out more details.
