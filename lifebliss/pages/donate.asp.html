Make a Blissful Difference!

Swamiji says "We need to adopt a life of nothing but gratitude. Thanking God does not mean visualizing empty space and thanking it in a jiffy! You need to start from thanking the people who built the hospital in which you were delivered from your mother's womb! People, plants and animals are all a part of God or Cosmos. If you feel only gratitude, there will be only happiness in your life. When there is true happiness in your life, good fortune will prevail. True Happiness or Ananda has the property of attracting good fortune."

Share your bliss with others by contributing to Swamiji's various charitable activities.

To make a donation to Life Bliss Foundation, please donate online or fill and mail the pledge form.

Please note:
  • All funds must be in U.S. dollars.
  • If paying by check, please print the pledge form and mail along with check.
  • Checks and money order (made payable to: Life Bliss Foundation), AMEX, Visa and MasterCard are accepted.
  • Your mailing address is essential to receive a receipt for tax purposes.
  • To make a Pledge, please complete the pledge form and mail it to us at
