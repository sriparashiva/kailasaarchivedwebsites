Life Bliss Meditation (Nithya Dhyaan) Schedule

For those hard-pressed for time, Nithyanandaji has cognized a 3-hour workshop called Life Bliss Meditation – Nithya Dhyaan. In this program, the participants learn a highly effective meditation technique which beautifully blends the timeless techniques from Patanjali Yoga Sutras to Tantra from Shiva Himself. This amazing workshop will introduce the essence of the inner sciences and the fundamentals of our body-mind system. A tiny step inwards could catapult you to discover amazing possibilities in every aspect of life.

 Acharyas trained directly by Nithyanandaji conduct these classes at various places in the United States and Canada. All programs are in English.

The workshops and other activities of the mission in Canada are organized and conducted by Nithyananda Meditation Academy, Canada.
