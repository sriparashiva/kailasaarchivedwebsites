Health & Wellness Program Schedule

The  Health & Wellness Program (HWP) is an intense two-day meditation program on the integrated science of body, mind and spirit with 14 meditation techniques. Acharyas trained directly by Nithyanandaji conduct these classes at various places worldwide. All programs are in English. Health & Wellness Program - Meditation is medication!

The workshops and other activities of the mission in Canada are organized and conducted by Nithyananda Meditation Academy, Canada. In all countries other than United States and Canada, all programs are conducted by Dhyanapeetam, India.
