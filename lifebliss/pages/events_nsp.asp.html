LBP Level 2 (Nithyananda Spurana Program) Schedule

The LBP Level 2 (Nithyananda Spurana Program) Nithyananda Spurana Program is a two-day meditation camp where participants have the rare opportunity to receive instruction and inspiration and explore in depth the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of our nature. We have the opportunity to consciously decide to fall in tune with our True Self.

The workshops and other activities of the mission in Canada are organized and conducted by Nithyananda Meditation Academy, Canada.
