A lady noticed that her third son’s appetite was increasing by the day. He came home one day and said, “Ma, the sandwiches are just not enough. Please keep some more.” She kept 6 instead of 4 sandwiches. The next day he came back and said that he wanted more. She kept 8 sandwiches. The following day, he asked for still more. She finally bought a long bread roll, slit it and filled it up. That day, he came back and said, “Ma, you’re back to one sandwich!”

Man is so obsessed with quantity that he totally misses the quality.
