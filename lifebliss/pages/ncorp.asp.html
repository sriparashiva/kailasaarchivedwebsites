Service Projects

Swamiji says, "The purpose of life is not two different things, Self realization and good of the world; they are one and the same.

When one is enlightened one speaks for the good of the world. When you start serving others, you are traveling towards yourself. Experience and expression are not different. When you experience you express; when you express you travel towards the experience. In service, start expressing compassion. You will travel towards experience; towards enlightenment. Service is the path to enlightenment; it is a part of enlightenment.

To serve you have to relate and reach out to the world. Stand up and take responsibility; that is the only way to show respect to the vedic system and lifestyle."
