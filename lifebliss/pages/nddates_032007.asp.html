Nithya Dhyan Schedule

For those hard-pressed for time, Nithyananda has cognized a 3-hour workshop called Nithya Dhyan – Meditation for Eternity. In this program, the participants learn a highly effective meditation technique which beautifully blends the timeless techniques from Patanjali Yoga Sutras to Tantra from Shiva Himself. This amazing workshop will introduce the essence of the inner sciences and the fundamentals of our body-mind system. A tiny step inwards could catapult you to discover amazing possibilities in every aspect of life.

 Acharyas trained directly by Swamiji conduct these classes at various places in the United States and Canada. All programs are in English.

The workshops and other activities of the mission in Canada are organized and conducted by Nithyananda Meditation Academy, Canada.

Charges:50 USD
