Nithyananda arrives in Los Angeles Ashram

February 26 2007

The first public darshan in the new meditation hall

Paramahamsa Nithyananda arrived in Los Angeles on February 26, 2007. During the upcoming Spring Tour, Nithyananda will be delivering public discoursesand conducting meditation workshops in 8 cities across the United States and Canada.

In the evening, the first public darshan was held in the new meditation hall/temple at the ashram. Over a 100 devotees gathered to welcome Nithyananda and receive his blessings.
