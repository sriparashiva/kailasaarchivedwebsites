Privacy Policy

lifebliss.org; LifeBlissGalleria.com: AnandaGalleria.com Revised June, 2007

Life Bliss Foundation is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its on-line users' Personal information. . Please read the following policy to understand the rules and procedures followed by Life Bliss Foundation in handling Personal information provided to us over the internet. If you have further questions regarding our privacy policy please contact us via email at legal@lifebliss.org.

What information does Life Bliss Foundation collect?
Life Bliss Foundation collects the Personal information only from persons who contact us and voluntarily provide the information to us or that is given to us by various affiliated Life Bliss Foundation organizations (satsangs and centers) that have your permission to share your Personal information. *Personal information* means your name, shipping/mailing address, telephone number, fax number, and email address and may include your program registration and donation preferences. We do not collect credit or debit card account numbers through our website. All financial transactions such as payment for our programs and donations done through our website are processed by Paypal, an independent third party online payment processing company. Users may review the terms of use and privacy policy for Paypal at www.paypal.com.

How Life Bliss Foundation uses the Personal Information
  1. We use your personal information to respond to and fulfill requests for products and services including: Sending information about the activities of Life Bliss Foundation, Nithyananda Foundation, Dhyanapeetam and our affiliates.
  2. Sending you our e-newsletters
  3. Contacting you about the status of your orders, donations and program registrations
  4. Completing your purchases and program registrations
  5. Completing your donations

When we disclose personal information to others
  1. Life Bliss Foundation does exchange Personal information with various affiliated organizations (satsangs and centers) in the U.S. and abroad so that they can contact people on the mailing list about Life Bliss Foundation's programs and events.
  2. Life Bliss Foundation's mailing list, including the Personal information we receive through the internet, is never shared with any commercial organizations and is never sold, rented or given to unrelated third parties.
  3. Life Bliss Foundation will disclose Personal information to third parties if we believe in good faith that the law requires us to do so.

Right to control and procedures to protect personal information
  1. Individuals who wish to register online for our programs and events are allowed to register online using the Register button on our website by providing their name, address, email id, and telephone number.
  2. Individuals who purchase goods or services from the Ananda Galleria online store or who give donations through our Life Bliss Foundation websites or who register and pay online for our programs are redirected to the Paypal website to complete the payment transaction. All financial information entered online is collected and processed by Paypal and Life Bliss Foundation does not collect, process, or retain any of the credit card information in our files. Please review the terms of use and privacy policy on the Paypal website. The only information maintained by us are your name, address, telephone number, and email address. Life Bliss Foundation limits access to your Personal information to those persons that are required to fulfill the requests for information, services or products. Your Personal information is protected with access only to those selected and authorized persons.

How we use Cookies
  1. Life Bliss Foundation does not use cookies to store information. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored in your computer's hard drive.
  2. When buyers place items in the shopping cart at anandagalleria.com, they are redirectd to a shopping cart engine powered by PaidShopCart. Users can review the privacy policy and user agreement of PaidShopCart at http://www.auctioninc.com/info/page/shopping_cart.

Regarding Children
  1. Life Bliss Foundation does not knowingly collect Personal information about or from persons under the age of 13.
  2. We do not sell or market products or services for purchase by children; however Dhyanapeetam does operate a program for children called Little Ananda's.
  3. Life Bliss Foundation website does not collect any Personal information from children who visit our site. Nor is any personal information collected from IP addresses, log files or other backend systems. Life Bliss Foundation and Dhyanapeetam may collect certain aggregate data, such as the number of users who visit the Web site, and may share that data with other sites owned and operated by Life Bliss Foundation or affiliated organizations.

Amendment of Policy
Life Bliss Foundation reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy any time. No individual notice of such changes will be provided but they will appear on the website in the form of a revised and newly dated Privacy Policy. Our current policy was amended June, 2007.
