Life Solutions

Life Bliss Foundation offers free discourses in various cities all overNorth America on topics related to meditation, bliss, love, peace, and harmony. These programs are offered free of charge to one and all. Nithyanandaji says: "Intellectual understanding along with meditation can spur you on to the super highways of integrated science. Man has to embark upon a journey inwards in order to understand the miracle - that is the phenomenon of the body, mind and spirit."

The Foundation is also engaging in taking its programs on Meditation and Yoga to

  • Juvenile halls
  • Prisons
  • Hospitals
  • Orphanages
  • Retirement Homes / Senior Centers
  • Law Enforcement Agencies

The programs and topics offer the public key insights into the message of meditation, bliss, love, peace, and healing. They are chosen to address specific problems faced by mankind in different parts of the world and provide a basis for

  1. Developing a deep understanding of a problem
  2. Providing a basis for dealing with the problem or emotion
  3. Techniques to handle and eliminate the problem.

They also provide tools to promote healthy living and spread bliss, love, peace, and harmony.
