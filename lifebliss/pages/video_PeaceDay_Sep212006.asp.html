United Nations International Day of Peace
Nithyananda Mission worldwide comes together for World Peace
Nithyananda says that it is time to heal the world by increasing collective positive energies through meditation and prayer. Keeping in line with the Master's message and mission, centers worldwide came together to contribute through the power of meditation towards the most pressing need of the hour for mankind...Peace on earth.
Bliss teams from India, USA, Australia, Canada, Malaysia, Taiwan, Singapore, Canada, Mexico, Brazil, Italy, Gaudeloupe(Caribbean) held 24-hour relay meditations or Mahasatsangs on this day...
