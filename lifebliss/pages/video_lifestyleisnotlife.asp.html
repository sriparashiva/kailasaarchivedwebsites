Lifestyle is not Life
We are so caught up with improving our lifestyle that we forget to live our lives! Your lifestyle determines all the external conditions of your life. Your life decides your inner experience. You can never fully control your external environment, but your internal state is always in your hands. Make this simple cognitive shift and your life will always be joyful no matter what!
