Sri Nithya Pranananda and Sri Nithya Gnanananda visit Toronto!

Posted on April 28, 2009

Sri Nithya Pranananda and Sri Nithya Gnanananda, direct disciples of Sri Paramahamsa Nithyananda, will be visiting Toronto.

Pranananda will be giving discourses related to Nithya Yoga and Vedic lifestyle and conducting Nithya Yoga sessions. (Stay tuned for additional details)

Sri Nithya Pranananda is one of the senior disciples of Sri Nithyananda Swami and is a highly accomplished teacher of Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam. He is one of the most senior teachers of Nithya Yoga and Hindu rituals. Pranananda has conducted more than 300 Nithya Yoga, meditation and Vedic rituals programs in India, Dubai and Malaysia. He conducts meditation programs to people from all walks of life from corporate employees to individual seekers. 		Gnanananda will be giving discourses related to meditation and conducting meditation workshops. (Stay tuned for additional details)

Sri Nithya Gnanananda is one of the senior disciples of Sri Nithyananda Swami and is a highly accomplished meditation and life solutions teacher of Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam. He is the present Spiritual Head of the Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam's Eastern Hemisphere Mission activities. Gnanananda is also the Head of the Nithyananda Publications, which publishes the spiritual teachings of Paramahamsa Sri Nithyananda.
