Guru Purnima Celebrations, July 7 2009

LIFE BLISS TREE, VANCOUVER, BC CANADA

Love,Bliss and Swamiji's Beautiful energy surrounded all of us on the Grand Day of Guru Purnima. Devotees took this opportunity to offer from their heart, Biksha, food and gorgeous flowers to Swamiji.

The Life Bliss Tree Center came Alive on this Blessed Guru Purnima day. To Start off our Nithya Yoga Acharya conducted a Nithya Yoga session. Then our ashramite lead a Guru Puja that made the whole Center so blissful. Next was the Maha Arathi beautifully administered by Ananda Sevaks and ashramites. WE celebrated with a Bharat Natyam dance performance done by a young devotee, followed by Nithya Dhyaan meditation, a DVD discourse by Swamiji and Finally Maha Prasad.
