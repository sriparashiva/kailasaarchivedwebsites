Privacy Policy

www.LifeBlissCanada.org; Revised February 2009

Nithyananda Meditation Academy is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its on-line users' Personal information. Please read the following policy to understand the rules and procedures followed by Nithyananda Meditation Academy in handling Personal information provided to us over the internet. If you have further questions regarding our privacy policy please contact us via email at LifeBliss.Vancouver@gmail.com.


Information Collected By Nithyananda Meditation Academy
Nithyananda Meditation Academy collects the Personal information only from persons who contact us and voluntarily provide the information to us or that is given to us by various affiliated Nithyananda Meditation Academy organizations (satsangs and centers) that have your permission to share your Personal information. *Personal information* means your name, shipping/mailing address, telephone number, fax number, and email address and may include your program registration and donation preferences. We do not collect credit or debit card account numbers through our website.


How Nithyananda Meditation Academy Uses the Personal Information
We use your personal information to respond to and fulfill requests for products and services including:

- Sending information about the activities of Nithyananda Meditation Academy.
- Sending you our e-newsletters, promotional offers about our products.
- Contacting you about the status of your orders, donations and program registrations.
- Completing your purchases and program registrations.
- Completing your donations.


When We Disclose Personal Information to Others
Nithyananda Meditation Academy does exchange Personal information with various affiliated organizations (satsangs and centers) in the Canada and abroad so that they can contact people on the mailing list about Nithyananda Meditation Academy's programs and events.
We use personal information to send promotional offers about our products.
Nithyananda Meditation Academy's mailing list, including the Personal information we receive through the internet, is never shared with any commercial organizations and is never sold, rented or given to unrelated third parties.
Nithyananda Meditation Academy will disclose Personal information to third parties if we believe in good faith that the law requires us to do so.

Right to Control and Procedures to Protect Personal Information
Individuals who wish to register online for our programs and events are allowed to register online using the Register button on our website by providing their name, address, email id, and telephone number.
Nithyananda Meditation Academy limits access to your Personal information to those persons that are required to fulfill the requests for information, services or products. Your Personal information is protected with access only to those selected and authorized persons.


How We Use Cookies
Nithyananda Meditation Academy does not use cookies to store information. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored in your computer's hard drive.


Regarding Children
Nithyananda Meditation Academy does not knowingly collect Personal information about or from persons under the age of 13.

Nithyananda Meditation Academy website does not collect any Personal information from children who visit our site. Nor is any personal information collected from IP addresses, log files or other backend systems. Nithyananda Meditation Academy may collect certain aggregate data, such as the number of users who visit the Web site, and may share that data with other sites owned and operated by Nithyananda Meditation Academy or associated organizations.


Amendment of Policy
Nithyananda Meditation Academy reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy any time. No individual notice of such changes will be provided but they will appear on the website in the form of a revised and newly dated Privacy Policy. Our current policy was amended February, 2009.
