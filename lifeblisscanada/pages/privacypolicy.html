Privacy Policy
lifeblisscanada.org; Revised October, 2008.

Life Bliss Canada is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its on-line users' Personal information. . Please read the following policy to understand the rules and procedures followed by Life Bliss Canada in handling Personal information provided to us over the internet. If you have further questions regarding our privacy policy please contact us via email at legal@lifebliss.org.


What information does Life Bliss Foundation collect?
Life Bliss Canada collects the Personal information only from persons who contact us and voluntarily provide the information to us or that is given to us by various affiliated Life Bliss Canada organizations (satsangs and centers) that have your permission to share your Personal information. *Personal information* means your name, shipping/mailing address, telephone number, fax number, and email address and may include your program registration and donation preferences. We do not collect credit or debit card account numbers through our website.


How Life Bliss Canada uses the Personal Information
We use your personal information to respond to and fulfill requests for products and services including: Sending information about the activities of Life Bliss Canada.
Sending you our e-newsletters, promotional offers about our products.
Contacting you about the status of your orders, donations and program registrations
Completing your purchases and program registrations
Completing your donations


When we disclose personal information to others
Life Bliss Canada does exchange Personal information with various affiliated organizations (satsangs and centers) in the U.S. and abroad so that they can contact people on the mailing list about Life Bliss Canada's programs and events.
We use personal information to send promotional offers about our products.
Life Bliss Canada's mailing list, including the Personal information we receive through the internet, is never shared with any commercial organizations and is never sold, rented or given to unrelated third parties.
Life Bliss Canada will disclose Personal information to third parties if we believe in good faith that the law requires us to do so.


Right to control and procedures to protect personal information
Individuals who wish to register online for our programs and events are allowed to register online using the Register button on our website by providing their name, address, email id, and telephone number.
Life Bliss Canada limits access to your Personal information to those persons that are required to fulfill the requests for information, services or products. Your Personal information is protected with access only to those selected and authorized persons.


How we use Cookies
Life Bliss Canada does not use cookies to store information. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored in your computer's hard drive.


Regarding Children
Life Bliss Canada does not knowingly collect Personal information about or from persons under the age of 13.

Life Bliss Canada website does not collect any Personal information from children who visit our site. Nor is any personal information collected from IP addresses, log files or other backend systems. Life Bliss Canada may collect certain aggregate data, such as the number of users who visit the Web site, and may share that data with other sites owned and operated by Life Bliss Canada or associated organizations.


Amendment of Policy
Life Bliss Canada reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy any time. No individual notice of such changes will be provided but they will appear on the website in the form of a revised and newly dated Privacy Policy. Our current policy was amended October, 2008.
