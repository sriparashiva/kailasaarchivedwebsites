Vision

Create an ideal civilization ‘living enlightenment’

a) Visionary executives - Energy to change that which needs to be changed - realize their dreams and achieve the peak of human potential

b) Intelligent realists - Intelligence to accept that which cannot be changed

c) Stress-free individuals - Techniques to realize and understand the deep secret of life - what we see as reality in Existence is a continously changing dream

d) Fulfilled beings - Deep devotion to the Unchangeable Ultimate

This leads to successful and peaceful beings living enlightenment. Lets join hands to nurture a blissful society
