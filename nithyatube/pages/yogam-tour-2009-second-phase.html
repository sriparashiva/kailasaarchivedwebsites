About This Blog


This is a video blog. Source material for this blog comes from youtube videos put up by user LifeBlissFoundation.


Main reasons for having this blog:
To categorize Nithyananda's discourse videos and hence have a catalog based on discourse topics.
To view videos on one page without bothering to keep track of Youtube's related video links.

Life Bliss Foundation now operates in 1200 centres in 33 countries and 30 Nithyananda Meditation Academies (NMA) around the world, to spread the message of peace and bliss through individual transformation.

Swamiji's discourses are now available in DVD and CD formats and for Ipod downloads at www.lifeblissgalleria.com.
