  "Go Green - Adopt a Tree" awareness campaign in partnership with National Geographic Channel

In an effort to spread awareness about the importance of green environment, the Nithyananda Youth Foundation NYF Bengaluru partnered with National Geographic Channel India’s GO GREEN campaign. Under this campaign, Adopt a Tree is the theme where plant saplings are offered for free for anyone willing to adopt and take care of them. NYF has taken up the responsibility of reaching out to people and encourage them to adopt a tree in the RBI Colony area of the garder city of Bengaluru.


Free Healing Camp and Nithya Dhyaan Meditation at Terapanth Sabha Bhavan, Gandhi Nagar, Bangalore - July, 2009

Members of the Jain community were introduced to Nithya Dhyaan meditation and Nithya Spiritual healing after the satsang on Sunday. The meditations were guided in Hindi and evoked a lot of interest in healing and the various benefits of meditation. The women and children experienced the healing performed by licensed Nithya Spiritual healers at the center.


Nithya Dhyaan Initiation and Healing Camp at Aranya Bhavan, Karnataka Forest Department, Malleshwaram - August 2009

Officials of the forest department were amazed at the beautiful transforming effects of Nithya Dhyaan during the meditation and healing camp conducted for them. The participants were introduced to the science behind the meditation techniques and physical, mental and spiritual benefits derived from practicing them.
