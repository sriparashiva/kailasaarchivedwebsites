IMPACT OF NITHYANANDA GURUKUL

An in-house survey of the Gurukul, revealed that the Gurukul had both an immediate and long-lasting impact on the lives of the children.

  • 89 percent of children are more committed to their education.

  • A phenomenal 100% of the parents of the children said the Nithyananda Gurukul was one of the best things available in the community and said they were happy to have their children in the school. 

  • 80 percent of the problem kids have transformed dramatically into well behaved, fun loving and focused children.
