  • Life - A Miracle after my Tryst With Swamiji

    It all started in July 2007, when I rushed to the Gynecologist with very slight pain in the pelvic, she put me on antibiotics and after two months in vain, suggested a biopsy. The diagnosis was Polypoidal Cervicitis. I was asked to undergo a hysterectomy. (removal of the uterus). I went in for a second opinion, she too suggested surgery.  

    I was amazed at my calm when I heard these words, the feeling within was so strong, I knew I would be healed. 

    I agreed outwardly for a surgery in December 2007, but waited calmly, as if something would happen. 

    BEFORE HEALING-
    I had pain from my hips which would extend to my feet. I could not stand continuously for more than 7 to 10 minutes. The pain would worsen terribly on some days, I would almost feel like giving up my resolution. I had no assurance that I would be healed, only the gut feel and faith. My stamina was failing. 

    During a public darshan at Swami’s program, he told me "We shall wait, we shall heal" (wait pannalam ma, heal pannalam ma.) His words were my weapon, my reassurance, my renewal of faith. I waited endlessly…………for what, I did not know. 

    Meanwhile, I meditated almost everyday and gave healing to myself. When I could, I would receive healing from some other healer. 

    It was around the 10th of March, I woke up one morning to find that my physical and mental energies had hit the bottom, the pain and fear were getting unbearable. I did not want to do anything, I literally dragged myself to work, once I was there I managed through the day, this would repeat every day. I heard that Swami was giving healing to the public from 1st March at the ashram. I could not carry on any longer, even my daily chores of cooking, etc., were a terrible effort and painful. I went to see Swami on the 15th March 2008 and stayed at the Ashram to receive healing for ten days. 

    DURING HEALING - 
    I have tried to put in words very briefly, what I experienced at the ashram, words are no substitute, I still cry and feel so touched when I go through my experiences every time. The banyan tree which I experienced as so loving, touching and healing, I felt like there was an army carrying me through and consoling me, pains and hunger would disappear, answers to many questions would roll in, I would space out, every moment was treasured, precious…………………., the loving and caring people around you, the music was soothing, tasty lunch, cooked so lovingly………………and the healing, caring, compassionate……………loving touch. Shiva sutras every evening, what more could one want in life I often mused. I yearned for a place for my tired soul to rest and he rested me in a haven which was heaven. 

    With all this, I was also brought to face my fears, my innate patterns, my doubts, my anger with myself, the inability to forgive myself, he held a mirror before me and I was looking within. I laid all this and more at his feet. 

    As each day progressed, there were differences in my body, the pain increased and spread to my whole body and then suddenly dissipated. I was overwhelmed with gratitude, I felt lighter and lighter as if I had lost a lot of weight, my stomach which had bulged due to all the wounds and infection, suddenly deflated like a balloon. On the last day, my clothes hung loose on me. I stood before Swami, with renewed confidence, no fear, contented for every moment that I had lived in the ashram, ready to take on life as it comes, energetic, enthusiastic, rejuvenated, full of love for every being and a love for life, the wisdom to accept what I could not change and the courage to change what I could. It did not matter to me if I had been healed or not, I had experienced such richness in every moment, pain or no pain, I had learnt many a lesson and am to learn many, many more. I bid a tearful farewell, though deep down I understood it was for me to find this bliss wherever I went. 

    AFTER HEALING- 
    I visited the doctor after I came back from the ashram, she was amazed with how I had been healed and certified that I was fine and was to visit her only after a year, there was no need for any surgery!

    I feel ten years younger now, more stamina, the pain that would extend from my hip to my legs disappeared, I am able to walk long distances, stand for longer hours and even dance. My awareness and consciousness has sharpened.

    I am much much more humane now, a child at heart, ……………………..in deep gratitude to all that I have received and learnt and continue to learn, from the time Swami came into my life six years ago. Gurubhyo namaha. Nithyanandam. 

    Ma Nithya Aparna
