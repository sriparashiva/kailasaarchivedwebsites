   About Us

Dhyanapeetam - All in just 6 years!

Vision: “Totality through transformation”

 Mission: “Leading the way to a fulfilled society that can go beyond the basic needs of Food, Healthcare and Education, thus evolving and ultimately raising the human consciousness”

NGI is a recently consolidated unit of the Dhyanapeetam Charitable Trust’s Community Development Wing. Central to the value system we adhere to at the Nithyananda Global Initiatives, is our commitment to community development and ultimately transformation at an individual, national and global level. In fact, this is the very essence of our vision and mission and the reason why we exist.

This commitment rests on the belief that all our efforts should substantially benefit the populace around us. Driven by this commitment, a number of projects in the fields of holistic health, education and many more, have been successfully implemented in the last five years. A number of radical initiatives are in the pipeline now, as we discover more and more opportunities to contribute to the betterment of the world we live in. Over the years we were able to meet the challenges and continuous learning has taken place. 

Accordingly, we are now better prepared to face the challenges of the task we have undertaken, especially with regard to the significance of sustainable development. A people centered, participatory and thoroughly professional approach is being adopted in all project implementations with the view to safeguard transparency and accountability.
