NITHYANANDA VIDYALAYAS
Curriculum

The Nithyananda Vidyalayas have a curriculum designed to foster Spontaneity, Creativity, Clarity and Use of intuition in individuals. All round development of Emotional Quotient, Intelligence Quotient and Spiritual Quotient is focused upon to give shape to persons with rich cross cultural growth and healthy respect for one and all.

The curriculum so includes:
  • Conventional education (Classes 1-10). 
  • Meditation, Nithya Yoga, Vedic education involving study of scriptures, chanting and rituals with scientific understanding.
  • Extra curricular activities
  • Core program areas
    • Character and Leadership Development
    • Knowledge and raising the Consciousness
    • Health and Life Science Skills; The Arts; Sports, Fitness and Recreation
