NITHYANANDA VIDYALAYAS
Our Success Story

Nithyananda Gurukul, Bidadi, Bengaluru.

The Nithyananda Gurukul was designed by Paramahamsa Nithyananda, to provide an ambience for children to blossom and flower and explore every dimension of their inherent inner potential thus entering into adulthood fully equipped not only with modern education but also the wisdom to realize and choose what they want to do in life. 

Established: October 2, 2006

Our Students: 50 students of different ages and different ethnic backgrounds. 

Following a Vedic system of education, Nithyananda Gurukul is a comprehensive strategy based on research showing that students do much better academically when they spend their non-school hours engaged in fun, educational activities. The strategy consists of the following components:

1. Homework help and tutoring

2. High-yield learning activities: integrating fun, traditional educational activities

3. Residential program to help completely focus on their growth in every dimension 

Nithyananda Gurukul is uniquely positioned to enhance the potential of students due to four characteristics:

1. The Gurukul is designed and operated exclusively for children, with the needs and interests of the young children in mind.

2. A residential education system where the students learn and play in the ambience of an ashram thus giving the teachers an opportunity to help them develop and grow in every dimension. 

3. The Gurukul has a core staff of trained professionals who provide guidance and supervision, and serve as role models.

4. The Gurukul is accessible and affordable to all.
