   Aptamitra - Youth Awakening Initiative 

Mission : Inspiration, Innovation, Integrity

A platform where young people achieve their full potential both in their inner consciousness and the outer world, Aptamitra has been conceived keeping in mind the emerging needs of today's youth. In the present day scenario, youth face a multitude of issues which demoralizes them and takes a toll on their otherwise undying spirit.

Aptamitra aims to harness this youthful energy to nurture integrated, responsible and dynamic individuals; who can feel the pulse of the moment and contribute to achieve a positive change in their community.

What we do:

Nithyananda Youth Foundation (NYF): Nithyananda Youth Foundation helps its members realize their full potential in terms of IQ, EQ and SQ. The Foundation imparts the blissful lifestyle of the Vedic Masters to create spiritual as well as material wealth. It trains the youth in life bliss skills that helps them stay Enlightened in all stages of their life. NYF makes the youth realize the immense significance of the spiritual infrastructure of India and its traditions, rituals that are pro-scientific and pro-life.

Life Bliss Technology is a free one-year residential program that has been initiated at the for youth aged between 18 and 30 years. The thrust of this program is to help youth from all types of socio-economic backgrounds to become self-supporting and confident in facing life and excelling in whatever field they may choose. It provides an intense atmosphere for the youth to grow in both inner and outer worlds.

What we can do:

• To assist and enable under-privileged youth in urban and rural areas to realize their full potential through innovative and interactive programs.

• Enhance holistic health of youth utilizing unique meditation techniques

• Provide skills to upgrade their financial capabilities, through Vocational Training Programs

• Dissipation of information regarding various careers through Career Guidance Cells

• Extend the support to youth in distress through professional Counseling Centers
