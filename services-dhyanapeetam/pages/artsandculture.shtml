   Encouraging Traditional Arts and Culture

Arts & Culture | Sangeeth Academy | Temple Arts | Vedic Teachings | Publishing | Youtube

Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust through its Nithyananda Global Initiatives encourages traditional art and culture as an underlying element of raising human sensitivity and consciousness. Leading and upcoming artists of traditional music and dance are encouraged to perform in all the festive occasions at the mission’s vedic temples and ashrams world over. To spread the rich Vedic culture, every opportunity is used to explain the significance and benefits of every activity – worship services, traditional music and dance, chanting of Sanskrit verses, temple and architecture forms, importance of devotion, dharma etc.
