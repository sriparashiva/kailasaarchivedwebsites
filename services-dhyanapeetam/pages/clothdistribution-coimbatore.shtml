   Clothes Distribution & Spiritual Awareness Program
        at Center for Positive People  - Coimbatore

December 13 20009, Coimbatore: The Women's Empowerment wing of NGI distributed new clothes & biscuits to children at Coimbatore Network for positive people (CNP+), which is an organization guiding and supporting people infected and affected by AIDS. Located at Kuniamuthur in Coimbatore, this organization is supported by Tamil Nadu State AIDS Control Society (TANSACS). A talk about the "Importance of Meditation" and an active meditation technique was also conducted for the people in the center.
