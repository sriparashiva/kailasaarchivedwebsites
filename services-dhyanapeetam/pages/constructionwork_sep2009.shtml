   Ashram Insfrastructure - Construction Work at the Bengaluru Ashram

Construciton work is in full swing at the Bengaluru ashram, in an effort to provide more and more facilities to the huge number of devotees visiting the ashram. Large meditation Halls to accomodate 10,000 people and other small meditation halls are being built. A 1000 bed dorm is under construction to accomodate the devotees who will be visiting the ashram.
