   Flood Relief Efforts - Inauguration of Shelters, AP

Initial Karnataka Flood Relief Pictures  |  Andhra Relief Pictures  |  Continuing Relief Operations in Karnataka  |  Andhra Relief Pictures 2nd visit   |  Andhra Reconstruction Pictures 3rd visit    |  Andhra Relief - Shed Inauguration
Dhyanapeetam is continuing its Flood relief work in effected areas in Kurnool and Mahboobnagar districts in Andhra Pradesh, helping the villagers to reconstruct their life and their houses. A total of 5 sheds have been built providing accommodation for 120 families. 

2 sheds were constructed in Madhur Village of Size 20 X 50 accommodating 15 families each, 2 sheds were constructed in Sangala Village of size 20 X 75 accommodating 30 families each and 1 shed was constructed in Rajoli Village of size 20 X 75 accommodating 30 families. The inauguration was done by an MLA of the district.
