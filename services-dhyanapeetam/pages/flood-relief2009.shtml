   Flood Relief Efforts - Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh

Initial Karnataka Flood Relief Pictures  |  Andhra Relief Pictures  |  Continuing Relief Operations in Karnataka  |  Andhra Relief Pictures 2nd visit   |  Andhra Reconstruction Pictures 3rd visit    |  Andhra Relief - Shed Inauguration
The second batch of relief material containing food packs, clothes for children and adults, blankets, plates and tumblers is making its way to the flood affected regions of Karnataka. 

The South Indian states of Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh are still reeling under the effects of the worst floods in a century. To help the people in this hour of crisis, a team of volunteers from Nithyananda Global Initiatives has begun Flood Relief Work in the affected areas. The ashram is in constant touch with the relief officer in charge, to ensure that the relief material reaches the flood victims.
