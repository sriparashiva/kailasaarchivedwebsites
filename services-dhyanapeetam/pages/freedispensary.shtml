   Free Dispensary

This further aids in speeding up healing, works on a deeper level than the physical level and improves the natural healing power of the body. Further information about this science of Nithya Energy Healing can be found in the publication of Nithyananda Publishers, titled ‘Mystic Phenomena and Scientific Data’. Additional research on Nithya Energy healing on Asthma, Pain and other diseases in through well known researchers from USA and India are underway. The allopathic medicines are state-of-the-art and approved by FDA and manufactured by reputed companies. We also receive discounts of upto 40% from the distributors who support us in the cause by providing the quality medicines at reduced cost. 

We offer alternative systems of medicine that include Ayurveda and Siddha systems that have proven their efficacy by the sheer practise of these medicines over 100's of years.
