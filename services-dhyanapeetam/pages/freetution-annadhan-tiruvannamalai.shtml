   Free Tuition for Underprivileged Children at Tiruvannamalai Ashram

Nithyananda Global Initiatives is conducting free tuition classes to over 100 underprivileged children. The service is provided at Tiruvannamalai ashram in Tamil Nadu everyday, assisting in their education. Healthy nutritional food is also provided to these children daily by the ashram Brahmacharis' who also take the classes for them.
