PROJECT BRIEF

Buddha gives a beautiful explanation or definition for the word ‘healing’. He says healing is our way of expressing our compassion, our care towards somebody to help restore his health to him.

Healing is nothing but helping a person restore his physical, mental and emotional wellbeing.

Good health is not just absence of disease. It is a presence of ease, a sense of wellbeing. We are not mere humans who are meant to fall sick and then get cured. We are embodiments of bliss. We were created to live with a blissful feeling at the being level all the time. This is what good health is meant to be.

Our body is like the hardware of a computer and the mind is like the software. When we get some disease, we address the physical problem. We try to fix the hardware but most of the time, it is the software that is the cause of the problem! The software or the mind is corrupted, so it continuously gives the wrong instructions to the hardware, the body. It is our mental setup that is the root cause of disease and imbalance in our body-mind system. Modern medicine acknowledges the influence of mind over many body related illnesses. Both body and mind related illnesses are caused by energy disturbances in the body.

WHAT IS NITHYA ENERGY HEALING

The science of giving energy to a person who is diseased, sick or who is feeling a lack of energy, is what we call healing.

Nithya Spiritual Healing is healing through cosmic energy. The cosmic energy is different from the energy that we know in various forms - such as electrical energy. Electrical energy does not have its own intelligence. If you put a light bulb into an electric socket, the current will flow and the bulb will glow. If you put your finger in that electric socket, the same current will flow but you will receive an electric shock! The electricity does not have the intelligence to determine what to do while flowing.

Cosmic energy is pure intelligence. The cosmic energy is the Intelligence that runs the whole cosmos. There is no cop standing to regulate the motion of planets and starts in the universe. Yet, they all move in an inherent order, driven by the cosmic intelligence. There are so many important involuntary functions happening in our body like breathing, digestion, blood circulation etc. All these activities happen beautifully without our intervention as they are driven by the cosmic intelligence. This same cosmic intelligence can hence heal our body-mind as well.

Nithya Spiritual Healing is a technique to channel this all-pervading intelligent cosmic energy through the healer, from Existence to the person in need of healing.

Further information about this science of Nithya Energy Healing supported by scientific research is available in the publication titled ‘Mystic Phenomena Scientific Data’. Additional research on Nithya Energy healing on Asthma, Pain and other diseases by well-known researchers from USA and India is underway.

The healing system works at the energy level and has been proven to be a powerful and effective alternative healing system that supplements conventional medical treatment. It aids in speeding up healing, works on a deeper level than the physical level and improves the natural healing power of the body.

Till now Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust has through the Nithya Energy Healing system, touched 3.5 million people worldwide.
