   Multi Specialty Hospital for Holistic Health Care 

Mission  : "Arogya"- A state of dis-ease-less-ness. Aiming towards a disease free, healthy society through a sustained and professional approach in providing holistic health care.

We propose to set up a full fledged, 200 bed, multi-specialty hospital to be implemented in two phases.

FACILITIES PHASE 1- 100 Bed, secondary care, multi specialty hospital with Yoga and allied medical sciences.

FACILITIES AND SERVICES: 

Out Patient Departments

  • Internal medicine 

  • General surgery

  • Orthopedics 

  • Obstetrician & Gynecology

  • Pediatrics 

  • Emergency services

  • ENT 

  • Ophthalmology

  • Dermatology 

  • Psychiatry

  • Endocrinology 

  • Dentistry 

  • Cardiology 

  • Neuro surgery

  • Radiology and ultrasound scan 

  • Oncology

  • Physio-therapy

 Yoga Center

  • Clinics

  • Counseling center

  • Yoga hall 

  • Rehabilitation camps

  • Utility area

Implementation in 24 months by Apr’2011

 
PHASE 2 - Addition of another 100 bed and tertiary care level with cardiology and ortho/ neuro as thrust area.

Additional facilities include: 

Specialized OPD, Intensive care units like- CCU and Cath lab Operation theatres for Cardiology and Ortho/ Neuro.

Implementation in 30 months byOct’2011
