   How We Work

The core of our organization is its volunteers. We work through our various ashrams and centers, which are run by dedicated volunteers. At present we have around 25 ashrams and hundreds of active centers around the globe. We are working on raising funds to help us reach out to more and more people across the globe.

Who is a typical Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust volunteer and what is the role of a Volunteer?

The core of Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust is its volunteers. The foundation runs on the strength of its volunteers. Volunteers are a team of dedicated people willing to give their time and skills in the service of humanity at large and raise the resources to help meet people's needs.
