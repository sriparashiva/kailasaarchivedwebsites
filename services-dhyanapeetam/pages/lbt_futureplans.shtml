Future Plans

Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust is expanding the program reaching out to more students across the country. The future scope for this project includes:

  • 2000 – LBT students to be served annually across different states and countries. 

  • 15 – LBT schools to be set up across different states, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Hyderabad, Uttar Pradesh. 

  • 1000 - Expand the operations to reach out to youth in slums and train separately for better life. 

  • 100 trained professional youth workers full time and part time
