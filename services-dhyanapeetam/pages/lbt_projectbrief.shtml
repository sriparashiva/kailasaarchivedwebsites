   Life Bliss Technology - The Technology of Blissful Living

Project Brief | Impact of LBT | Future Plans
It is time to save the planet earth by creating more and more people who are awakened, enlightened. Unless we create a critical mass of enlightened people, we can't think of saving the planet earth, because so many calamities are happening around the world, which are created by the unconscious human beings - global warming, HIV, religious terrorism, fanaticism and so many atomic weapons getting piled. We are facing today so many dangers.

We need to create a peaceful, blissful, joyful understanding and an enlightened society, enlightened humanity to save planet earth. This will lead to inner world success and outer world productivity.

In my nine years of wandering, I searched for a place where I could learn under one umbrella the technology of blissful living. But I never found such a place. I found the knowledge in bits and pieces in various parts of the country. That is why it took me nine years to pick up the technology.

When I attained inner bliss, the first thing I wanted to do was make available this technology for the next generations under one roof. Out of this inspiration, I created the Life Bliss Technology which provides an ambience to help anybody pick up the technology of life bliss."

- Paramahamsa Nithyananda

The Life Bliss Technology program for youth was founded in 2004. This is a free 3 months/2 years in-house residential training program on practical life skills and sciences, helping youth to expand their horizons and showing them a new dimension to life.

Over 300 students have taken part in this intense self-development program, of which 248 students felt so touched and inspired that they decided to stay back and help the foundation in its mission to serve humanity. The other 52 students went back and found jobs in a life made richer in the internal and the external world.

Currently, the foundation takes care of more than 300 LBT students at its centers across the globe. It imparts the students with the training in their chosen field while providing food, shelter, clothing and money to help them lead a comfortable life.  

PROJECT BRIEF

Envision a world where all young people achieve their full potential both in their inner consciousness and the outer world, thus shaping their own future with clarity and self-confidence. Imagine a world run by this same youth who have grown into honest & peace loving, energetic & dynamic individuals; who can feel the need of the people and work tirelessly to achieve a positive change in their community.



LBT aims to improve the conditions and prospects for young people: where they live, learn, work, and play, we also teach them on how to tap into their latent intelligence and energy with a unique blend of scientific methods and traditional techniques.
 
With the support of the foundation, young adults from different states in India and other countries are developing marketable skills in the area of meditation therapy and life solutions; school dropouts are becoming young entrepreneurs; gaining leadership skills; organizing and executing skills in varied fields like Travel study programs, music concerts, e-commerce, book publishing houses, hotel management and much more. The spiritual and vocational training extended to these young adults provide them with the resources or expertise to effectively address the challenges facing today’s young people. The thrust of the program is to help youth from all types of socio-economic backgrounds to become self-supporting and confident in facing life and excelling in whatever field they may choose.

Programs for youth provide support and services in all areas of their personal development that include yoga and meditation, vocational training, health education, cultural tolerance, environmental awareness, and the development of leadership, conflict resolution, and decision-making skills. 

The following four areas form the core of the foundations youth activities. 
 
Education: to improve the quality of learning, helping young people transform into emotionally and intellectually strong adults. The foundation also provides learning opportunities for young people through expanded access to information technology, instructional support from qualified individuals and freedom to experiment in varied fields giving a free reign to their creativity.  

Employability: to improve young people's employment, entrepreneurial, and personal skills as a way to build their capacity for and engagement in productive work.

Leadership: to inspire, support, and promote youth engagement and the role of young people as leaders of positive social change, as a way to foster a lifelong commitment to active citizenship. 

Health Education: to teach and prepare the youth to lead healthy lives by providing them with the knowledge and personal skills needed to make informed and healthy choices.

The program is designed to transform today’s youth to a well-rounded individual with:

  • A solid inner balance

  • Extensive learning

  • Strong leadership quality

  • Raising the intelligence quotient, emotional quotient and spiritual quotient of the youth

  • Training in different vocational skills like computer aided design, audio/video capturing and processing, organizing and conducting events and public speaking.

  • The ability and confidence to be economically self-sufficient

  • Creative intelligence and spontaneity

  • Good physical and mental health

  • Ability to maintain smooth inter-personal relationships

  • The capacity to actualize one’s potential
