   Medical Camps

Mission : "Arogya"- A state of dis-ease-less-ness. Aiming towards a disease free, healthy society through a sustained and professional approach in providing holistic health care.

WHAT WE DO

We offer free general medical check-up camps with free consultation and diagnosis by qualified doctors, free medicines and follow-ups in Bidadi and surrounding villages. Such camps are being conducted in many areas of Tamil Nadu as well.

We also conduct Specialty health camps in the areas of gynecology, ophthalmology etc using allopathy as well as complementary systems of medicine like homeopathy, Ayurveda, siddha and acupuncture. 

Beneficiaries: 400 patients in 3 villages, four times per month 

WHAT WE CAN DO

• There is an urgency to provide additional medical services like advanced diagnostics including scans, gynecological tests, ECG, sugar test, cancer detection tests etc as an integral part of our Community Outreach Program. There is a necessity to expand the service to atleast 150,000 needy people residing in rural areas in the states of Karnataka, Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh

• Various Mental Health facilities such as Family Counseling centers and De-addiction centers are the need of the hour as stress creeps into homes.

 

As a part of World Aids Day, Nithyananda Youth Center conducted the AIDS Awareness Camp at Viveka Matriculation School, Sendamangalam, South India
