   Meditation Centers

Mission : "Arogya"- A state of dis-ease-less-ness. Aiming towards a disease free, healthy society through a sustained and professional approach in providing holistic health care.

WHAT WE DO

Nithya Spiritual Healing centers, Meditation centers, Formal courses in Meditation as therapy, are groundbreaking initiatives by the Trust to facilitate individual transformation.

WHAT WE CAN DO

Formal membership In Meditation Therapy (FIMT) graduates, Life Bliss Technology graduates and Nithya Spiritual Healers will engage in setting up of numerous Counseling centers and Nithya Spiritual Healing Centers, in different localities so that people can benefit from these services.
