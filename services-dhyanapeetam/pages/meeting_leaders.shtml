   Meeting Leaders for Global Peace and Harmony

Nithyananda meets renowned spiritual and social leaders worldwide, discussing the need to raise the individual consciousness to cause collective consciousness to enter into the super-conscious zone. Strongly recommending meditation as a sure solution, Nithyananda creates ties with the leaders in areas of education, energy healing, green drives, world peace, meditation and yoga to benefit humanity on a greater scale.

Following are a few pictures of Nithyananda with global leaders.
