   FREE Medical Camp - Dec 30 2008

Dhyanapeetam Charitable Trust in collaboration with Sri Adichunchanagiri Medical College and Hospital organized and conducted a Free multi specialty medical camp at the Dhyanapeetam ashram at Bidadi.
 
A strong team of 150 doctors came together offering their service for free checkup, diagnosis, treatment and follow up treatment (which includes ambulance services,  surgery, hospital stay services) for the following diseases:
 
  • Asthma
  • Bone problems
  • Diabetes
  • Arthritis
  • Blood diseases
  • Eye problems
  • ENT
  • Depression
  • Child specialists
  • Anemia
  • Cataract
  • Hernia
  • Stones
  • Chicken Pox
  • TB
  • Dental problems
  • Heart Problems
  • Woman's problems
  • Infertility problem
  • Uterus cancer
 
Other Free services that were provided are:
 
  • Scanning
  • ECG
  • Blood tests
  • Urine test
  • Medicines
  • Blood Bank
  • Follow up surgery & treatment at the hospital where Free bed and food will be provided
  • Ambulance
  • Surgery
