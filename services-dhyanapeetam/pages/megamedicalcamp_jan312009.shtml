   Multi Speciality Medical and Limb Donation Camp - Bengaluru

December 31 2009, Bengaluru: A mega multi specialty medical camp was conducted by Nithyananda Global Initiatives on Dec 31st 2009. Over a 1000 people were treated for various medical issues, by a large team of doctors, specialists, technicians and nurses from well know organizations. Some of the specialties that were offered were Dental (filling, extraction, complete denture and removable denture with follow up at the hospital), Pediatrics, Gynecology, Cardiology (with ECG), Eye, Urology, Gastroenterology, Injection administration, Orthopedic, General medicine, Skin specialists, holistic treatment like Homeopathy, Ayurveda, Siddha and Verma therapy. The medical camp also included a blood donation drive and spiritual healing services.

Earlier during the day an artificial limb donation camp was also held where over a 100 people received artificial limbs. Many of the people, who availed of the free medical services, expressed deep gratitude for the service and requested for more such camps since they were not able to afford the hospitals in the city.
