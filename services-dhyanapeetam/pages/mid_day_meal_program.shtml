   Mid Day Meal Program - Nithya Anna Daan

Mission: Mid Day Meal Program to help Eradicate hunger and Illiteracy among underprivileged Children 

Raju as this little kid is fondly called by his friends and family visits the Nithyananda Anna Mandir during meal time every day. His parents are construction workers living on daily wages barely making ends meet for their family of 6. Weak and undernourished when they first came to the ashram, they soon decided to stay and find a job in the ashram. Today, the whole family enjoys unlimited free food 3 times a day and is maintaining good health.

Nithya Anna Daan addresses the following:

Hunger
School meals help in breaking the cycle of hunger and poverty.

Health & Nutrition
Balanced nutritious meals offer important nutritional benefits to the growing child fulfilling their daily calorie requirement which plays a vital role in boosting their IQ and EQ contributing to their holistic growth.

Education
School meals encourage poor households to send their children to school helping them to become better citizens of the country
