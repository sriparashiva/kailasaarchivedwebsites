   Our Mission Vision & Beliefs

Our Mission

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.”

Take responsibility for issues faced by the common man of the world, thereby giving everyone the opportunity to realize themselves.

Our Vision

A world where people can live a healthy and blissful life physically, emotionally and spiritually. 

Our Beliefs

Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust believes in creating a long lasting sustainable transformation. That’s the reason why we address the issues holistically and spiritually right at the grassroots level. We believe that the solutions to the issue lies not just in the direct interventions, but beyond as well. Therefore, our focus on free meditation camps, energy healing and counseling. Through our efforts we believe we can bring about an irrevocable transformation in the society.
