   Our Vision Mission & Beliefs

Our Vision: “Totality through transformation"  

The NGI is working towards guiding and enabling people to experience a state of totality through transformation at the individual and societal level. ”

Our Mission: “Leading the way to a fulfilled society that can go beyond the basic needs of Food, Healthcare and Education, thus evolving and ultimately raising the human consciousness”

The mission engages in providing basic needs to human beings and empowers them to raise their standards of living so that they experience a sense of satisfaction following which exploring other dimensions of themselves becomes possible. The mission inspires people to take responsibility for issues faced by the common man of the world, thereby giving everyone the opportunity to realize themselves.

Our Beliefs

NGI believes in creating a long lasting sustainable transformation. Precisely the reason why we address issues holistically right at the grassroots level. We believe that the solutions to issues lie not just in the direct interventions, but beyond as well. Therefore, our focus is primarily on people’s empowerment and through our efforts we believe we can bring about an irrevocable transformation in the society.
