   Health At Your Doorstep - Mobile Health Vans

Mission : "Arogya"- A state of dis-ease-less-ness. Aiming towards a disease free, healthy society through a sustained and professional approach in providing holistic health care.

WHAT WE DO

Through mobile health vans we have been able to serve a larger population in the last three years. 

Beneficiaries: 12,000 patients in 24 villages every three months 

WHAT WE CAN DO

As the number of people who require this service is ever increasing, we wish to increase the number of mobile health vans so that this service is accessible to needy rural folk
