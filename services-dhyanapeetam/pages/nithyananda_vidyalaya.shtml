Mission : Fostering in the young an "indestructible spirit" through Nithyananda Vidyalayas.

NITHYANANDA VIDYALAYAS
In response to a need for high quality and holistic education, Nithyananda Vidyalayas enable "Enlightenment Based Education", to create an ideal civilization "Living Enlightenment"
  • We propose to establish 108 Nithyananda Vidyalayas with an average enrollment of 1000 students per school by the year 2012.
  • 100,000 children per year will be given Jeevan Mukthi mental setup
  • 6 hrs per day of conventional education system; 2 hrs per day of spiritual education
  • Teachers trained personally by Paramahamsa Nithyananda in Vedic system of learning

Profile of Students:

Nithyananda Vidyalayas are modeled on the "Gurukul" tradition of imparting knowledge, effectively sculpting physically healthy, mentally sharp and spiritually mature individuals. A five dimensional program has been designed with the ultimate aim of molding well groomed, highly intelligent and employable citizens.

The students who are groomed at Nithyananda Vidyalayas will be:

1. Visionary Executives: As individuals who express energy to change that which needs to be changed, students can confidently realize their dreams and achieve the peak of their potential.

2. Intelligent Realists: Facilitate the flowering of intelligence to accept that which cannot be changed after which individuals can face life realistically

3. Stress Free Individuals: Techniques imparted to individuals to understand the deep secrets of life and experience that Existence, in reality, is a continuously changing dream.

4. Fulfilled Beings: Enable individuals to cherish a deep devotion to the unchanging ultimate

5. Live Enlightenment: All these dimensions once explored will establish individuals in living enlightenment
