    ERADICATE HUNGER - HELP CREATE A STABLE FAMILY

Raju as this little kid is fondly called by his friends and family visits the Nithyananda anna Mandir during meal time every day. His parents are construction workers living on daily wages barely making ends meet for their family of 6. Weak and undernourished when they first came to the ashram, they soon decided to stay and find a job in the ashram. Today, the whole family enjoys unlimited free food 3 times a day and are maintaining good health.

The World Food Program estimates that a shocking 963 million people worldwide are undernourished, an almost unfathomable number. Wherever there is poverty there is Hunger all over the world. Urge your colleagues, friends and family to recognize The Right to Food.
