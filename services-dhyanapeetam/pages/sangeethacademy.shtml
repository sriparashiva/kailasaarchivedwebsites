   Nithyananda Sangeeth Academy

Arts & Culture | Sangeeth Academy | Temple Arts | Vedic Teachings | Publishing | Youtube

Nithyananda Sangeeth Academy offers learning music through the internet. It was inaugurated on 20 April 2008 in the Bangalore ashram by Swami. The Academy provides courses where the training is provided by eminent artists and professors.

“Nithyananda Sangeeth Academy, besides imparting knowledge and training in music, is unique as it envelops you with the spirit of the vedic times where learning happened under the grace of enlightened beings.” 
- Ma Nithyananda Keertana, Vice Principal, Nithyananda Sangeeth Academy
