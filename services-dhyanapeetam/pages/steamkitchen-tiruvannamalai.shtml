   New Mechanized Kitchen at Tiruvannamalai Ashram

Free food is served to everyone who visits the Tiruvannamalai ashram; an ashram that's run completely by Brahmachari's. Hot and nutritious satvic food is served 24 hours a day to more than a 1000 people. During the recent Karthikai Deepam festival more than half a million people visited the ashram and more than 60,000 people enjoyed the free meals that was cooked and served by the ashram  Brahmachari's. 
 
In order to provide a better service along with healthy hygienic food to the millions of people who visit the ashram, a new mechanized kitchen with steam boilers was installed recently.
