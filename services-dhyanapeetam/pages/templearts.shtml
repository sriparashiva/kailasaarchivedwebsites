   Nithyananda Temple Arts Preservation & Research Society

Arts & Culture | Sangeeth Academy | Temple Arts | Vedic Teachings | Publishing | Youtube

The Society preserves and spreads the ancient temple art forms, sculptures and architectures by encouraging and providing employment to the traditional artisans of this field. The exquisite pieces of art like deities made of metal, stone, wood etc are sent worldwide to the Vedic temples and to Nithyananda Sacred Arts which supplies these to the people at large.

The Society provides avenues for highly skilled artisans who serve to keep the ancient traditional art forms alive. This service is being provided to preserve this ancient art and provide a basic standard of living to artisans. Working with immense dedication and love, these artisans produce striking figures of deities and temple structures with intricate workmanship. They strive to preserve and reproduce the rich Vedic temple tradition over thousands of years old through the grandeur of stone, wood and metal carvings.
