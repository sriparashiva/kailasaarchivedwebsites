   What Makes Us Unique

  • We serve at different levels. We serve at different levels, not being determined by political will but the needs of the people we are trying to reach.
  • We work for people who are deprived; deprived physically, emotionally or spiritually.
  • All our social service activities are driven by volunteers.
  • Our approach is inclusive. We provide an opportunity for everyone to participate in our mission to serve the people’s needs.
  • We are scrupulous about observing the highest standards of accountability and transparency.
