   What we do

Ask not what your community can do for you..
Ask what you can do for your community!

At Dhyanapeeta Charitable Trust we try to address the needs of the people at the grassroots level. We believe in directly getting involved with the society and we believe that this is the only way one can effectively address the needs of the people and bring about long term and sustainable changes in their lives. We understand the needs of people coming from all walks of life including poor, middle class and rural families and have been working hard to address their physical, intellectual and spiritual needs. 

The past 3 years, since we came into existence, under the guidance of an Enlightened  Master we have been involved in social services that encompass a wide range of activities  across the field of children's education, medical diagnosis and treatment for the poor and  downtrodden, self development education for the youth, free food distribution, free medical dispensary, old age homes, life counseling, free meditation camps, to name a few. We offer  holistic systems of medicine that have been the treasures of wisdom and 5000 years of  research in the inner science. We also strive to preserve the ancient sculptures and sacred  art forms by supporting and encouraging the artisans who have years of experience with  techniques which have been passed down from generation to generation.

Dhyanapeeta Charitable functions on a grass-roots level, relying on community involvement and the support of innumerable volunteers and donors. Through this website, we invite you to learn more about us and join us in our efforts to serve the various needs of the needy in India.
