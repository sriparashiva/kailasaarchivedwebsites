   Women's Empowerment (Asha Kiran)

Mission : Towards Success Through Self-Reliance

Significant to the evolution of mankind in general and society to be specific, women power is a largely untapped resource. In order to harness this huge potential lying latent, efforts are being made to carve out a niche for women to recognize their self-worth and blossom into empowered and self-reliant members of the community.

Asha-Kiran through its programs aims to provide space and guidance to women who wish to pursue and fulfill their dreams. A trained team of volunteers will be involved in the outreach programs.

Ashraya (Haven): A radical initiative designed to lend a helping hand to underprivileged and marginalized women across all sections of society.

Ashraya focuses on creating opportunities for independent beings (women who have lost their spouses) to live a dignified life through training programs which will enable them to be economically self sufficient and thus enjoy a healthy self esteem.

Through this initiative, we plan to extend our support to at least 100 women in the next year. Most of the women will be from the lower-economic strata of the population who have no viable alternatives to lead fruitful lives.

Future Project : 

Literacy for women with special emphasis on the girl child.

Non- Formal education will be imparted to enable women to have access to basic and functional education. Many underprivileged women today are unable to even put their signatures. The need is to enable them to understand the functioning of banks, post offices and other infrastructure which play a significant role in their everyday lives. Though simple, in doing so, women can upgrade and rise above their "dependent" status to play a more pro- active role in their families. 

Legal aid will also be provided to women who need it through eminent lawyers.
