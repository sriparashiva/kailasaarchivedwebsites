Ganesha Pooja Celebration, Bidadi
- 3rd September 2008

 
 
  • Beautifully decorated Ganesha idol
 
  • 108 Ganesha display in the temple in ashram
 
  • Gurukul kids decorated a Ganesha idol and it is carried to the temple for pooja
 
 
  • Arati in progress
 
  • Ganapathy Homa starts in the presence of Swamiji
 
  • A group of 11 Brahmacharies of Nithyananda order conducted the homa
 
 
 
  • Swamiji watching the proceedings
 
 
  • At the end of Ganapathy Homa Swamiji blessed each and everyone present in the temple
