Donate to the Anna Mandir (Food Temple) serving free food to all devotees and visitors
Food is God. Serving food is a spiritual practice. In the glorious tradition of Indian culture, free food is served all through the day to all devotees and visitors in the form of Prasad (energized blessed food offered to the Divine) at the 30 Anna Mandirs around the world. Meals are also served in schools, colleges and to the poor and needy in rural areas. Mobile Anna Mandirs serve meals in Mass meditation camps. On average, we are serving 10,000 meals a day in our Anna Mandirs around the world.

Support Sacred Arts Preservation

This project strives to preserve ancient temple arts to produce awe-inspiring temple art works. Deities, temple structures, puja items and more, are producted through dedicated and skilled artisans, with the guidance of veteran sthapathis (professionals in temple arts). The entire work is based on Agama Shastra, the ancient science of temples and deities. Due to ardent efforts, an entire gamut of art forms related to temples, is being brought back to life from a state of near extinction. The lives of hundreds of skilled artisans have been given a new fillip and fresh direction.
