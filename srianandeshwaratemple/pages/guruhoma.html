Guru Homa, Mantra, Arathi (GMA) training :

This 3-day program is designed to offer training in performing

  • Guru homa - fire ritual done as an offering to the Guru
  • Mantra training - chanting of sanskrit verses while offering gratitude, offering food, for peace, while performing holy bath to idols etc.
  • Arathi - offering of camphor flame to Existence, as prayer and gratitude.

This program exposes one to the art of creative decoration of idols that symbolically represent Existence, the method of performing abhisheka or holy bath to idols, and several methods of pooja or offerings to Hindu Gods.

It instills a firm conviction in each individual about Existence and its glory. It lays the foundation for sincerity and commitment towards Existence as a whole and instigates love and devotion towards the Whole, thus helping each individual fall in tune with the Cosmos.

Those who have attended an ASP may attend this program. You may contact the ashram for more details and registration.
