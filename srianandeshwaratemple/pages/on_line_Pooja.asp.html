Pujas are a popular way to connect with god and uplift the inner space. The Anandeswara Anandeswari Temple consecrated by the Living Enlightened Master is located within the ambience of the energy hub - the 600 years old banyan tree and Swayambu Linga. The temple itself is located within the Ashram which in itself is a Live Energy field of the Master. The deities in the temple are energized to shower compassion upon the prayers of the devotees. Far or near prayers done with devotion is heard. Based on the traditional Vedic system rituals are performed in the temple all days at all specified times. The temple is an open place and welcomes everyone to come and experience the divine grace.

Online Pujas

If you unable to come to the ashram to offer puja in person, you may still get puja's and abhishekams conducted in your name at Sri Anandeshwara temple by registering through online puja. 

Wherever you may be, chant the guru mantra 'Om Hreem Nithyanandaya Namaha' between 7am/pm and 7:20 am/pm to be blessed and healed by Swamiji's energy.

Guru Puja:
Puja offered to the Guru on every Thursday as a thanksgiving.
The devotees can donate for the puja through cheque/cash/DD. On the specific day of worship the devotees can come and witness the puja in person. If they are unable to come the Prasad will be sent by courier to their respective postal address.

Abhisheka:
Number of Abhishekas are performed to Lord Shiva who is known to be a lover of Abhisheka. During conch Abhishekam for example mantras are chanted for each and every conch individually along with rudra chanting

Flower Decoration:
Anandeshwara & Anandwswari, Swayambu Linga and Dakshina Murthy:
The devotees can pay for a day’s decoration,  sponsoring a day’s flower decoration.

Pradosha puja:
Abhishekam is performed for Ananda Nandi and Swayambu Linga between 4.30-6.00 pm (during the Pradosha time)

Full Moon / New Moon Puja:
Performed for fulfillment of specific desires on two days - the day on which the full orb of the moon is seen and the day on which there is no moon, in a lunar month. The energy on this day is usually very high. A procession of Anandeshwar and Anandeshwari is carried around.

The Pujas are performed to the Deities by the Brahmachari's of the Ashram for the devotees:-
Please note the following points
1. If you want to offer the service on a particular date please mention the same in the Message column provided in the Registration Page.
2. Also if you request the service on a particular date please make sure your payment is received 3 weeks prior to the date of the service
3. Please note that it may not always be possible to perform the puja on the requested date (some days of the year are dedicated to special functions during which the individual online puja requests may not be performed) - if you are particular that the service are to be performed only on a particular day then please check with the temple before making the payment.
4. To request multiple service (Puja / Arathi) Please register for each service one after another.
