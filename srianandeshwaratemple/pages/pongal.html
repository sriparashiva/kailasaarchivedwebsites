Pongal at Bidadi, India 
- January 14 to January 17, 2008

Traditionally celebrated at harvest time, Pongal is a celebration of the prosperity associated with the harvest by thanking the rain, sun and the farm animals that have helped in the harvest. Pongal means "boiling or spilling over." The act of boiling over, of milk in the clay pot, is considered to denote future prosperity.
