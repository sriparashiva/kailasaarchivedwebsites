Bidadi, India: Purnabhisheka
January 17, 2008:

On Jan 17th, Master performed Purnabhisheka (a kind of holy bath) for a group of disciples, a 9 month Brahmacharya Initiation at the end of which, the Master decides what is best for the disciple - the Yoga or the Tantra path.  This spiritual custom of the Master pouring holy water on the disciple is done to help integrate one's body, mind and spirit.
