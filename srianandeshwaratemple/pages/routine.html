Daily Schedule

The day starts off with Suprabhatam in the morning.The dieties are awakened in the traditional way, arathi performed, and then they are taken to the Sanctum Sanctorium with ritualistic drum beats. A Holy bath is performed for Sri Anandeshwara( Swayambu Linga). The Nithya Dhyan Meditation is done by all the ashramites and the devotees present. This is followed by Guru Puja and the distribution of prasad. In the evening the Arathi is performed in the Temple.

 
   
   

At 10pm, the Artha jama Puja is performed. The dieties are carried from the Sanctum Sanctorium to the room where they are put to sleep with all festivities. An offering of milk and fruits and an arathi is also performed for the dieties. The dieties are then wrapped in a blanket as if they were alive.
