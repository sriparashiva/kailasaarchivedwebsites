Sapthayaham...

Sapthayaham is a 2-day program that includes a grand and festive procession of deities which can be conducted in a place of your request. Six deities along with Swamiji's throne will be brought from the ashram, decorated and poojas offered to. Each of the deities represents one of the seven chakras (subtle Energy centers) in the body and the throne represents the Sahasrara or the crown chakra. The chakras are closely related with our physical and emotional well-being. Each chakra is associated with a particular disease. When the chakras are kept in an energised condition, they cause good health and when they are low in energy, the corresponding disease surfaces.

The Sapthayaham radiates the Divine Energy in the space where it happens and heals people of physical and mental disorders.

Full Moon / New Moon Puja Performed for fulfillment of specific desires on two days - the day on which the full orb of the moon is seen and the day on which there is no moon, in a lunar month. the energy on this day is usually very high. A procession of Anandeshwar and Anandeshwari is carried around.
