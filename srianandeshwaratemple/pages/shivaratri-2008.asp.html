Maha Shivaratri ~ Shivam  - Bidadi, India 
- March 6, 2008

Maha Shiva Ratri, a very special festival for all disciples and devotees of Paramahamsa Nithyananda, was celebrated on a grand scale on March 6 2008 at the Bidadi ashram. The day saw a large crowd of people coming from all over the country and abroad to be with, celebrate and enjoy the presence of the Master. The day started with Master delivering the Shiva Sutras to a packed audience followed by a special program with healers and Ananda Samajites. The evening started with cultural performances by various popular dancers and artists from South India like Anuradha Sriram, Unnikrishnan, Ghatam by Dr. S Karthik to name a few. There was also a wonderful dance performance and Nithya Yoga asana demonstration by the Kids from the Nithyananda Gurukul. Master then released new books and  DVDs and launched the Tamil weekly magazine 'Nithyam Anandam' all published by Nithyananda Publication. The Master then performed the Maha Abhishekam at the Anandeshwara temple in the ashram where an offering of milk, flowers, fruits, rice, sandal, turmeric, rice flour, viboothi (sacred ash),  108 pots of energized water and rose water was made to the Shiva Linga. The night was filled with the sacred chants of Om Namah Shivaya while the Master performed the Rudra Homa, a fire ritual where various grand offerings is made to Shiva in the form of fire, which included 108 different kinds of herbs, flowers, sweets and silk sarees. The Homa was followed by a special initiation of  Brahmachari's who have completed 9 months of Purnabhisheka vows  successfully, a rare sight watched by thousands of blessed devotees. Master gave personal blessings until 8am the next morning to more than 7000 devotees who came from far and wide to spend this special day with the Master.
