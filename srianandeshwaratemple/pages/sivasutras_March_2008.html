Month of Shiva Sutra Discourses - Bangaluru, India  
- March 1 to 31, 2008

Shiva Sutras are 108 direct techniques to Enlightenment given by Shiva to Devi. They are direct solutions to every conceivable type of man who has come and gone and yet to come. They are solutions that take you beyond words, into a space where you can discover yourself. Shiva Sutras are for those who are tired of words and want to experience the Truth. They are shortcuts to the Ultimate Experience - to Enlightenment itself!

All of March 2008, Paramahamsa Nithyananda will be giving discourses on the Shiva Sutras. These gems of wisdom delivered by the Master has inspired and uplifted millions across the globe. His talks give a new meaning, a new understanding to the idea of enlightenment applicable to the modern day society. This talks are FREE and open to everyone who wants to come and listen to the Masters words.
