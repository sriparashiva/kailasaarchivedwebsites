Welfare Poojas

Guru Puja: Puja offered to the Guru on every Thursday as a thanksgiving.

The devotees can send the amount for the puja through cheque/cash/dd, On the specific day of worship the devotees can come and witness the puja in person. If they are unable to come the prasad will be sent by courier to their respective postal address.

Lord shiva is known as a lover of abisheka, thats why a number of abishekas are performed. During conch abishekam mantras are chanted for each and every conch individually along with rudra chanting

Flower Decoration for Anandeshwara & Anandwswari, swayambu linga, Dakshina Murthy. The devotees can pay the amount for a days decoration, thereby sponsoring that days flower decoration.

Pradosha puja- Doing abishekam for nandi and swayambu linga between 4.30-6.00 pm(pradosha time)

Full Moon / New Moon Puja Performed for fulfillment of specific desires on two days - the day on which the full orb of the moon is seen and the day on which there is no moon, in a lunar month. the energy on this day is usually very high. A procession of Anandeshwar and Anandeshwari is carried around.
