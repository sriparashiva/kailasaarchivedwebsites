Krishna Jayanthi at Bidadi, India 
- August 23, 2008

Sri Krishna's Birthday (Krishna Jayanthi) has been celebrated in the presence of Swamiji in a grand manner at Nithyananda Dhyanapeetam headquarters, Bidadi. The day started with the beautiful alankar (decoration) of Anandeshwar & Anandeshwari followed by evening enacted plays like Raasa Leela, Mahabharatha, Bhakta Prahaladha performed by our Brahmacharies and group performance by Gurukul kids added flavor for the whole program.

 
  • Anandeshwar decorated as Krishna on the eve of Krishna Jayanthi
 
  • Gurukul kids performs a group activity on Krishna Jayanthi celebration
 
 
  • Brahmacharies performs the Villupatu program
  • Gurukul kids enacts the role of Yasodha and Krishna
 
  • ... and some more activities
 
  • Krishna Leela performance and a scene from Mahabharatha (the great epic)
 
  • Story of Baktha Prahaladha adds up the program list
  • Gurukul kids in a group song
 
  • The beautiful doll decorated as Krishna to watch the program
