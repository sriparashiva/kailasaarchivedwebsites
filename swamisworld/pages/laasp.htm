Instructions for Los Angeles ASP Retreat Registration:

1. Completely fill out the registration information on below right.

2. Click on the 'Submit' button below

3. Click on the 'Continue' button on the following screen

4. If you wish to make payment by credit card, return to this page and click on the 'Buy Now' button. Follow the instructions to make payment via paypal.com

5. If you marked cash or check payment methods, you will be contacted as to how and when to make that payment.

6. After successfuly completing the registration, you will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.
