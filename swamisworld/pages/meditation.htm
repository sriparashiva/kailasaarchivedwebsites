Directions to Hindu Temple of Oklahoma City: From I-35 North or South Take Wilshire Blvd Exit. Make a left at the end of the ramp if you are coming from I-35 South. Make a right at the end of the ramp if you are coming from I-35 South. In about ½ mile you will see Coltrane on your right. Make a right on Coltrane. Proceed for about ¼ mile. Hindu Temple is on your left hand side. The gate to the Hindu Temple has an "OM" sign. Also, the board to the Hindu Temple says "ICF - Indian Cultural Foundation".
